Property Management Operations Manual

Define your portfolio and get a complete property management operations manual - tenant screening, maintenance workflows, financial reporting, and legal compliance.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into any LLM. Use the output as your property management SOP, whether self-managing or delegating to a property manager.

The Prompt

You are a property management executive who manages 5,000+ units with 95%+ occupancy rates, minimal evictions, and high tenant satisfaction scores.

Portfolio size: [NUMBER OF UNITS]
Property types: [SINGLE FAMILY/MULTI-FAMILY/COMMERCIAL/MIXED]
Management: [SELF-MANAGED/PROPERTY MANAGER/BUILDING OWN PM COMPANY]
Location(s): [STATE(S) - FOR LEGAL COMPLIANCE]
Biggest pain point: [MAINTENANCE/TENANTS/VACANCY/ACCOUNTING/COMPLIANCE]

Create a complete operations manual:

**1. Tenant Acquisition**
- Listing template (photos, description, key info)
- Showing process (self-showing vs in-person, scheduling)
- Application requirements and process
- Screening criteria (credit, income, rental history, criminal)
  - Fair Housing compliance checklist
  - Adverse action notice template
- Lease agreement key terms
- Move-in process and inspection checklist
- Security deposit handling (state law compliance)

**2. Tenant Relations**
- Communication protocols (response times by channel)
- Rent collection process (due dates, grace period, late fees)
- Lease renewal process (timeline, rent adjustment methodology)
- Complaint handling procedure
- Lease violation notice templates
- Roommate and subletting policy
- Pet policy and pet screening

**3. Maintenance Management**
- Emergency vs urgent vs routine classification
- Emergency: response protocol (water leak, no heat, lockout)
- Work order system (submission, assignment, tracking, closure)
- Vendor management (approved vendor list, bid process, insurance requirements)
- Preventive maintenance schedule (HVAC, plumbing, roof, pest, landscaping)
- Make-ready process (unit turnover checklist)
- Capital improvement planning (annual budget, reserve study)

**4. Financial Management**
- Rent roll tracking
- Operating budget template (by property and portfolio)
- Monthly financial reporting (P&L, cash flow, variance analysis)
- Accounts payable process
- Reserve fund management
- Tax preparation support (1099s, depreciation schedules)
- Owner distribution schedule and reporting

**5. Legal Compliance**
- Fair Housing: 7 protected classes + state additions
- Lease compliance monitoring
- Eviction process (notice types, timelines, court procedures)
- Security deposit return requirements (timeline, itemization)
- Property inspection laws (notice requirements)
- Lead paint disclosure (pre-1978 properties)
- Local rental registration and licensing
- Insurance requirements (landlord, tenant, umbrella)

**6. Vacancy and Turnover**
- Notice-to-vacate processing
- Move-out inspection process and form
- Security deposit reconciliation template
- Unit turnover timeline (target: 3-7 days)
- Turnover cost tracking
- Re-listing and marketing launch

**7. Technology Stack**
- Property management software (recommendation by portfolio size)
- Accounting integration
- Maintenance request system
- Tenant communication platform
- Electronic lease signing
- Online rent payment setup
- Smart lock and access management

Provide actual templates, checklists, and scripts. Mark items that require state-specific legal review.

Pro Tips for Real Estate

  • Ask the AI to include specific financial calculations with formulas shown so you can verify the math independently.
  • Include comparable property data when available so the AI can provide market-aware recommendations.
  • Request jurisdiction-specific legal considerations since real estate laws vary dramatically by state and municipality.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You are preparing a comparative market analysis and need a structured approach to pricing a property accurately.
  • You are listing a property and need compelling description copy that drives showings and offers.
  • You are evaluating a potential investment property and need a comprehensive financial analysis before making an offer.

Expected Results

  • Step-by-step operational playbooks with scripts, templates, and follow-up sequences.
  • Legal-aware documentation templates with jurisdiction-specific compliance reminders.
  • MLS-ready listing descriptions with emotional hooks, feature highlights, and SEO keywords.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Swap the financial figures with your real purchase price, rates, taxes, and rental estimates.
  • Adjust the target buyer or investor profile to match your specific market and property positioning.
  • Include your local market data (days on market, comparable sales, inventory levels) for accurate analysis.
