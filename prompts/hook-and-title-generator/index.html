Hook and Title Generator

Describe your content and get 10 scroll-stopping title options plus 3 opening hook variations to grab readers in the first sentence.

How to Use

Copy the prompt and describe your content topic and target audience.

The Prompt

Generate titles and opening hooks for content about [TOPIC].

Target audience: [AUDIENCE]
Platform: [Blog / Newsletter / LinkedIn / YouTube / Twitter]
Tone: [Professional / Casual / Provocative / Inspirational]

Provide:
1. 10 title options using different formulas:
   - 2 using numbers/lists
   - 2 using questions
   - 2 using strong claims or contrarian takes
   - 2 using curiosity gaps
   - 2 using benefit-focused language

2. 3 opening hook variations:
   - One starting with a surprising stat or fact
   - One starting with a relatable story or scenario
   - One starting with a bold statement or question

Each title should be under 70 characters and optimized for clicks without being clickbait.
