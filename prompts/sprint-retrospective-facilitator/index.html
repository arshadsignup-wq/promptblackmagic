Sprint Retrospective Facilitator

Share your sprint goals and outcomes. Get a structured retrospective with what worked, what failed, root causes, and action items.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Enter your sprint details and team observations. Use the output to run a focused, productive retrospective meeting.

The Prompt

Facilitate a sprint retrospective analysis for my team.

Sprint details:
- Sprint number/name: [SPRINT IDENTIFIER]
- Sprint goal: [WHAT WE SET OUT TO ACHIEVE]
- Duration: [1 WEEK / 2 WEEKS / 3 WEEKS]
- Team size: [NUMBER OF PEOPLE]
- What we completed: [LIST COMPLETED ITEMS]
- What we did not complete: [LIST INCOMPLETE ITEMS]
- Unexpected issues: [ANYTHING THAT CAME UP MID-SPRINT]

Team observations (optional):
[PASTE ANY FEEDBACK FROM TEAM MEMBERS]

Generate a structured retrospective:

1. What went well (keep doing):
   - Identify 3-5 wins with specific reasons why they worked
   - Highlight individual or team behaviors worth reinforcing

2. What did not go well (stop doing):
   - Identify 3-5 problems with root cause analysis (use "5 Whys" technique)
   - Distinguish between process problems vs. people problems vs. external factors

3. What to try next sprint (start doing):
   - 3-5 specific, actionable experiments (not vague goals like "communicate better")
   - Each action item should have: owner, deadline, and success criteria

4. Velocity analysis:
   - Was our estimation accurate? Where did we over/underestimate?
   - Capacity planning suggestions for next sprint

5. Team health check: Based on the patterns, rate team morale and suggest one team-building action

6. One-page summary I can share with stakeholders (non-technical language)

Pro Tips for Project Management

  • Ask the AI to create milestone-based project plans with clear deliverables and dependencies between tasks.
  • Always specify the project scope, team size, and timeline when prompting for project management guidance.
  • Ask the AI to identify potential risks and blockers upfront. Proactive risk management prevents 80% of project delays.

When to Use This Prompt

  • Your project is behind schedule and you need a recovery plan that identifies which deliverables can be descoped or fast-tracked.
  • You are managing a cross-functional team and need communication protocols that keep remote and in-office members aligned.
  • Your stakeholder wants a status report and you need a concise update that highlights progress, risks, and upcoming milestones.

Expected Results

  • Project charter documents with scope definitions, RACI matrices, and success criteria.
  • Risk registers with probability ratings, impact assessments, and mitigation action plans.
  • Status report templates with progress metrics, blockers, and upcoming milestone visibility.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Modify the methodology references to match what your organization uses: Agile, Waterfall, or hybrid.
  • Swap the sample milestones with your actual deliverables and their acceptance criteria.
  • Replace the project type placeholder with your actual project: software launch, marketing campaign, or office move.
