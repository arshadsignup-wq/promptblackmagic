Real Estate Listing Photo Enhancer

Upload property details and get AI prompts for virtual staging, twilight shots, aerial views, and lifestyle photography for real estate listings.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into your preferred LLM with property details. Use the generated prompts in AI image tools for virtual staging.

The Prompt

You are a top real estate photographer and virtual staging expert who has photographed luxury properties for Sotheby's and Christie's.

Property type: [HOUSE/CONDO/APARTMENT/COMMERCIAL]
Style: [MODERN/TRADITIONAL/FARMHOUSE/INDUSTRIAL/COASTAL]
Key rooms: [LIST THE ROOMS TO PHOTOGRAPH]
Target buyer: [FIRST-TIME BUYER/LUXURY/INVESTOR/FAMILY]
Price range: [PRICE BRACKET]

Generate detailed AI image prompts for:

1. **Virtual Staging** - For each room listed, create a prompt that adds furniture, decor, and styling appropriate to the property's style and target buyer. Specify exact furniture pieces, rug styles, art, plants, and lighting fixtures.

2. **Twilight Exterior** - Golden hour/blue hour exterior shot with warm interior lights glowing, landscaping enhanced, sky replacement if needed.

3. **Aerial/Drone View** - Bird's eye establishing shot showing property, yard, neighborhood context.

4. **Lifestyle Vignette** - Scene that helps buyers imagine living there (morning coffee on the patio, family dinner, reading by fireplace).

5. **Before/After Enhancement** - Take a plain room description and create prompts for both the empty version and the staged version.

For each prompt include: camera angle, lighting (natural vs enhanced), color temperature (warm and inviting), composition rules, and specific prop/furniture brands or styles to reference. Format for Midjourney with aspect ratios for MLS listings (3:2) and social media (4:5).

Pro Tips for Image Generation

  • Always specify the exact camera, lens, and aperture in your prompt - AI image generators produce dramatically better results with technical photography terms.
  • Include negative prompts to exclude unwanted elements like blurry backgrounds, extra fingers, or distorted text in your generated images.
  • Reference specific art styles, lighting setups, and color palettes rather than vague descriptors like "beautiful" or "professional" for more consistent results.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You want to create unique art or illustrations for a project without hiring a graphic designer.
  • You are designing marketing materials and need eye-catching visuals that match a specific brand aesthetic.
  • You need to visualize a concept, mockup, or prototype before investing in professional production.

Expected Results

  • Multiple variations of the same concept covering different styles, angles, and moods for A/B testing.
  • Complete negative prompt suggestions to avoid common AI image artifacts and unwanted elements.
  • Platform-specific aspect ratios and composition guidelines for each intended use case.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Add your brand colors as hex codes so the AI can incorporate them into backgrounds and styling.
  • Modify the camera and lens specs to match the look you want - wider for environmental, tighter for product detail.
  • Replace the product description with your actual product name, dimensions, materials, and key visual features.
