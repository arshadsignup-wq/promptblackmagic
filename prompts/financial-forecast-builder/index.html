Financial Forecast Builder

Provide your business numbers and get a 12-month revenue forecast with expense breakdown, cash flow projection, and key assumptions explained.

How to Use

Copy the prompt and fill in your current business financials.

The Prompt

Build a 12-month financial forecast for my business.

Current numbers:
- Monthly revenue: [AMOUNT]
- Monthly expenses: [AMOUNT]
- Growth rate (month over month): [PERCENTAGE]
- Customer acquisition cost: [AMOUNT]
- Average revenue per customer: [AMOUNT]
- Current customer count: [NUMBER]

Provide:
1. Month-by-month revenue projection (table format)
2. Expense breakdown by category
3. Cash flow forecast showing when you hit profitability
4. Key assumptions behind each projection
5. Best case, base case, and worst case scenarios
6. The 3 levers that have the biggest impact on the forecast
7. Warning signs to watch for that mean projections are off

Be realistic, not optimistic. Use conservative estimates.
