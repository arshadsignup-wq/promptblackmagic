Personal Finance

Investment Portfolio Review

Share your investment holdings and goals. Get an analysis of your asset allocation, diversification gaps, and rebalancing suggestions.

How to Use

List your current investments and financial goals. The AI reviews your portfolio and suggests improvements.

The Prompt

Review my investment portfolio and suggest improvements. You are not providing financial advice, just educational analysis.

My situation:
- Age: [YOUR AGE]
- Risk tolerance: [CONSERVATIVE / MODERATE / AGGRESSIVE]
- Investment timeline: [YEARS UNTIL YOU NEED THE MONEY]
- Monthly amount I can invest: $[AMOUNT]
- Retirement accounts: [401K / IRA / ROTH IRA / NONE]
- Employer match: [YES, UP TO X% / NO]

My current holdings:
[LIST YOUR INVESTMENTS, e.g.:
- S&P 500 index fund: $X
- Individual stocks: [LIST THEM WITH VALUES]
- Bonds: $X
- Cash/savings: $X
- Crypto: $X
- Real estate: $X]

Analyze:
1. Current asset allocation (stocks vs. bonds vs. cash vs. alternatives) as percentages
2. Compare my allocation to recommended allocation for my age and risk tolerance
3. Diversification check: am I too concentrated in any sector, geography, or asset class?
4. Am I maximizing my employer match? If not, calculate how much free money I am leaving on the table
5. Tax efficiency: are my investments in the right account types?
6. Rebalancing suggestions: what to increase, decrease, or add
7. A simple monthly investment plan going forward

Disclaimer: This is educational, not financial advice. I should consult a qualified financial advisor for personalized decisions.
