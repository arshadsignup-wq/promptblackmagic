Map and Cartography Illustration Prompt

Describe a real or fictional location and get AI prompts for illustrated maps - from fantasy RPG maps to vintage travel maps to modern city guides.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Use with Midjourney or DALL-E 3. Great for book illustrations, game design, travel blogs, or wall art.

The Prompt

You are a cartographic illustrator who creates hand-drawn maps for Tolkien-estate-quality fantasy books, National Geographic features, and luxury travel brands.

Location: [REAL CITY/FICTIONAL WORLD/BUILDING INTERIOR/TRAIL/REGION/CONTINENT]
Map purpose: [FANTASY RPG/TRAVEL GUIDE/HISTORICAL/EDUCATIONAL/DECORATIVE/GAME]
Style: [HAND-DRAWN INK/VINTAGE AGED PARCHMENT/MODERN ILLUSTRATED/SATELLITE ARTISTIC/MEDIEVAL]
Include: [TERRAIN/BUILDINGS/ROADS/LABELS/COMPASS ROSE/LEGEND/SCALE/SEA MONSTERS]

Generate 5 map illustration prompts:

**1. Full Regional/World Map**
- Birds-eye view of the entire area
- Terrain features (mountains as side-view illustrations, forests as tree icons)
- Water bodies with wave patterns or line shading
- Settlement markers with named labels
- Compass rose and decorative border
- Aged parchment or clean paper background

**2. City / Town Close-Up Map**
- Individual buildings visible (isometric or top-down)
- Street names and district labels
- Key landmarks highlighted and illustrated
- Parks, water features, bridges detailed
- Legend explaining symbols
- Walking routes or tour paths marked

**3. Cross-Section / Elevation Map**
- Side view showing terrain elevation changes
- Underground features (caves, tunnels, mines)
- Vegetation changes with altitude
- Labeled peaks, valleys, and water crossings
- Scientific illustration meets artistic design

**4. Treasure / Adventure Map**
- Weathered, stained parchment look
- X marks the spot with dotted path
- Cryptic symbols and coded legends
- Torn edges and fold lines
- Coffee-stained and hand-annotated feel
- Hidden details and Easter eggs

**5. Decorative / Wall Art Map**
- Designed to be framed and displayed
- Artistic license over geographic accuracy
- Beautiful typography for place names
- Illustrated vignettes of key locations
- Color palette coordinated for interior decor
- Border design matching the era/theme

For each prompt: specify whether North is up, the projection style, label language, and specific landmarks or features to include. Include art style references (Tolkien maps, vintage Ordnance Survey, Google Maps illustrated) and color palette.

Pro Tips for Image Generation

  • Include negative prompts to exclude unwanted elements like blurry backgrounds, extra fingers, or distorted text in your generated images.
  • Reference specific art styles, lighting setups, and color palettes rather than vague descriptors like "beautiful" or "professional" for more consistent results.
  • Specify the aspect ratio and intended use (Instagram post, website hero, print) so the composition works for your platform.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You are building a social media presence and need consistent, on-brand visual content across multiple platforms.
  • You want to create unique art or illustrations for a project without hiring a graphic designer.
  • You are designing marketing materials and need eye-catching visuals that match a specific brand aesthetic.

Expected Results

  • Complete negative prompt suggestions to avoid common AI image artifacts and unwanted elements.
  • Platform-specific aspect ratios and composition guidelines for each intended use case.
  • Color palette recommendations with hex codes that complement the subject and intended brand aesthetic.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Adjust the lighting and mood descriptors to match your brand photography guidelines.
  • Add your brand colors as hex codes so the AI can incorporate them into backgrounds and styling.
  • Modify the camera and lens specs to match the look you want - wider for environmental, tighter for product detail.
