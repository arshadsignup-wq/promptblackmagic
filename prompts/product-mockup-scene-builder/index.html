Product Mockup Scene Builder

Name your product type and get AI prompts for professional mockup scenes - from packaging on shelves to devices in lifestyle settings.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Use with Midjourney or DALL-E 3. Fill in your product details and preferred setting.

The Prompt

You are a commercial product photographer and CGI artist who creates mockup scenes for global brands like Apple, Nike, and Aesop.

Product type: [PACKAGING/DEVICE/BOTTLE/BOOK/APPAREL/CANDLE/COSMETICS/FOOD]
Product dimensions: [APPROXIMATE SIZE]
Brand aesthetic: [PREMIUM/PLAYFUL/ORGANIC/TECH-FORWARD/VINTAGE]
Primary use: [E-COMMERCE LISTING/SOCIAL MEDIA/PITCH DECK/PACKAGING DESIGN REVIEW]

Generate 6 mockup scene prompts:

**1. Clean Studio Shot - White Background**
- Product centered, perfectly lit
- Soft shadow beneath for grounding
- Multiple angles: front, 3/4, side
- E-commerce ready (Amazon/Shopify standard)
- High key lighting, no distracting elements

**2. Lifestyle Flat Lay**
- Product surrounded by complementary lifestyle items
- Carefully curated props that tell a story about the user
- Overhead view on textured surface (marble, linen, wood)
- Color-coordinated arrangement
- Negative space for text overlay

**3. In-Use / Lifestyle Context**
- Product being used naturally in its intended environment
- Hands or person interacting with the product
- Soft, natural lighting (window light feel)
- Warm, aspirational setting
- Shallow depth of field drawing focus to product

**4. Grouped / Collection Display**
- Multiple products or variants arranged together
- Size relationships clear
- Unified visual language across the collection
- Gradient or stepped arrangement
- Clean but dynamic composition

**5. Detail / Texture Close-Up**
- Macro view of product surface, label, or unique feature
- Shows quality and craftsmanship
- Material rendering: glass clarity, paper texture, metal finish
- Dramatic side lighting creating depth
- Color accuracy is paramount

**6. Seasonal / Campaign Theme**
- Product styled for a specific season or campaign
- Environmental props (autumn leaves, beach sand, snow, flowers)
- Color grading matching seasonal mood
- Gift-giving or celebration context
- Social-media-ready composition

For each prompt: specify exact lighting setup, surface material, prop suggestions, color temperature, and camera angle. Write as single-paragraph prompts optimized for AI image generators.

Pro Tips for Image Generation

  • Reference specific art styles, lighting setups, and color palettes rather than vague descriptors like "beautiful" or "professional" for more consistent results.
  • Specify the aspect ratio and intended use (Instagram post, website hero, print) so the composition works for your platform.
  • Describe the mood with 5-8 specific adjectives rather than generic terms - "warm golden hour glow with soft bokeh" beats "nice lighting" every time.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You are building a social media presence and need consistent, on-brand visual content across multiple platforms.
  • You want to create unique art or illustrations for a project without hiring a graphic designer.
  • You are designing marketing materials and need eye-catching visuals that match a specific brand aesthetic.

Expected Results

  • Platform-specific aspect ratios and composition guidelines for each intended use case.
  • Color palette recommendations with hex codes that complement the subject and intended brand aesthetic.
  • Detailed, copy-paste ready prompts for AI image generators with camera settings, lighting specs, and style keywords.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Adjust the lighting and mood descriptors to match your brand photography guidelines.
  • Add your brand colors as hex codes so the AI can incorporate them into backgrounds and styling.
  • Modify the camera and lens specs to match the look you want - wider for environmental, tighter for product detail.
