YouTube Channel Growth Strategy

Define your channel niche and get a complete YouTube growth strategy - content pillars, upload schedule, SEO, thumbnail design, and monetization plan.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into any LLM. Use the output to plan your next 90 days of YouTube content and growth tactics.

The Prompt

You are a YouTube growth strategist who has helped 50+ channels grow from 0 to 100K subscribers using data-driven content strategy and algorithm understanding.

Channel niche: [YOUR TOPIC/INDUSTRY]
Current subscribers: [COUNT]
Upload frequency: [CURRENT AND TARGET]
Monetization goal: [AD REVENUE/SPONSORSHIPS/PRODUCTS/COURSES/ALL]
Biggest growth obstacle: [LOW VIEWS/LOW CTR/LOW RETENTION/NO CONSISTENCY/WRONG AUDIENCE]

Create a complete YouTube growth strategy:

**1. Channel Positioning**
- Niche definition (specific enough to own, broad enough to grow)
- Target viewer persona (who, why they watch, when they watch)
- Channel value proposition ("This channel helps [who] to [what] by [how]")
- Competitive analysis (5 competing channels, gaps, opportunities)
- Your unfair advantage (what you can do that others can't)

**2. Content Pillars (4-5 types)**
- Pillar 1: Discovery content (searchable, how-to, SEO-driven)
- Pillar 2: Trending/timely (news, reactions, current events in your niche)
- Pillar 3: Community content (audience requests, Q&A, collaborations)
- Pillar 4: Tentpole content (big productions, series, viral attempts)
- Pillar 5: Shorts (repurposed clips, original short content)
For each: example titles, frequency, production effort, growth function

**3. Title and Thumbnail System**
- Title formulas that drive clicks:
  - Curiosity gap: "I Tried [X] for 30 Days - Here's What Happened"
  - Number + benefit: "7 [Tools] That Will [Benefit]"
  - Emotional trigger: "Stop [Doing This Mistake] Right Now"
- Thumbnail design principles:
  - 3 elements max (face, text, visual)
  - High contrast colors that pop in browse
  - Face with emotion (if personality-driven channel)
  - Text: 3-5 words max, readable at mobile size
  - A/B testing approach for thumbnails

**4. SEO and Discovery**
- Keyword research process (tools: vidIQ, TubeBuddy, YouTube autocomplete)
- Title optimization (primary keyword + emotional modifier)
- Description template (keyword-rich first 2 lines, links, timestamps)
- Tag strategy (broad + specific + long-tail)
- Hashtag usage (3 max)
- Playlist strategy for binge-watching
- End screen and card placement

**5. Retention Optimization**
- First 30 seconds: hook formula (see video script prompt)
- Pattern interrupts every 60-90 seconds
- Story structure for long-form content
- B-roll and visual variety to maintain interest
- Chapter/timestamp strategy
- Analytics review: where viewers drop off and why

**6. Upload Schedule and Workflow**
- Content calendar (3 months planned, 2 weeks ahead in production)
- Batch filming process (film multiple videos in one session)
- Editing workflow (software, templates, outsourcing decisions)
- Publishing schedule (best days and times for your niche)
- Shorts publishing strategy (frequency, repurposing)

**7. Community and Engagement**
- Comment strategy (first hour after upload, pinned comments)
- Community tab content ideas
- Collaboration framework (who to collab with, how to propose)
- Social media cross-promotion (don't just share links)
- Email list building from YouTube

**8. Monetization Roadmap**
- Pre-monetization: build the audience (0-1K subscribers)
- Ad revenue (1K subs + 4K watch hours threshold)
- Sponsorship: when to start, how to pitch, rate card
- Products and courses: when to launch, what to sell
- Affiliate marketing: natural integration, disclosure
- Revenue diversification timeline

Provide specific, actionable tactics, not generic advice. Include video ideas ready to produce.

Pro Tips for Video & Multimedia

  • Request B-roll and visual direction notes alongside scripts so you have a complete filming guide.
  • Include your equipment setup (phone vs camera, mic type, lighting) so production recommendations match your capabilities.
  • Specify your platform (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels) since optimal format, length, and style differ dramatically.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You are starting a YouTube channel and need professional scripts that keep viewers watching until the end.
  • You want to grow on TikTok or Instagram Reels but do not know what content formats work best for your niche.
  • You are launching a podcast and need everything from concept to launch strategy to growth tactics.

Expected Results

  • Production workflows for batch-creating content efficiently with minimal equipment.
  • Complete video scripts with timestamps, B-roll directions, on-screen text cues, and retention hooks.
  • Content calendars with 30 days of specific ideas, hooks, captions, and posting schedules.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Adjust the video length and format to match your platform requirements and audience preferences.
  • Include your current subscriber or follower count so growth tactics match your stage.
  • Modify the production notes based on your actual equipment and editing capabilities.
