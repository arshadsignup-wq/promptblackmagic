Cold Email Sequence Writer

Describe your product and target buyer. Get a 3-email outreach sequence with subject lines, personalization tips, and follow-up timing.

How to Use

Copy the prompt and fill in your product and prospect details.

The Prompt

Write a 3-email cold outreach sequence.

My product: [WHAT YOU SELL]
Target buyer: [JOB TITLE AND COMPANY TYPE]
Key pain point we solve: [MAIN PROBLEM]
Best proof point: [CASE STUDY, STAT, OR SOCIAL PROOF]

For each email provide:
- Subject line (under 50 characters, optimized for open rate)
- Preview text
- Email body (under 100 words each)
- Clear CTA
- When to send relative to the previous email

Email 1: Initial outreach (lead with their pain point)
Email 2: Follow-up with proof (send 3 days later)
Email 3: Breakup email (send 5 days after email 2)

Rules:
- No generic openers ('I hope this finds you well')
- Each email should stand alone (assume they did not read the previous one)
- Include a personalization placeholder [PERSONAL NOTE] in each
- Keep it conversational, not salesy
