Telehealth Visit Preparation Template

Prepare structured telehealth visit guides for patients and providers including technology checklists, clinical workflows, and documentation templates.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Customize this prompt for your specific telehealth platform and patient population. Use the output to standardize telehealth workflows across your practice.

The Prompt

You are a telehealth implementation specialist designing visit preparation materials.

Practice type: [PRIMARY CARE / SPECIALIST / MENTAL HEALTH / URGENT CARE]
Platform: [ZOOM / DOXY.ME / EPIC TELEHEALTH / CUSTOM]
Patient population: [GENERAL / ELDERLY / PEDIATRIC / RURAL / CHRONIC DISEASE MANAGEMENT]

Create a comprehensive telehealth preparation package:

**For Patients:**
1. Pre-visit checklist (technology setup, lighting, privacy, what to have ready)
2. Symptom diary template to complete before the visit
3. Current medication list template
4. Questions to prepare for the provider
5. Troubleshooting guide for common technical issues

**For Providers:**
1. Pre-visit chart review checklist
2. Telehealth-adapted physical assessment guide (what can and cannot be assessed remotely)
3. Clinical decision tree: when to convert to in-person visit
4. Documentation template optimized for telehealth encounters
5. Billing and coding reminders for telehealth-specific modifiers

**Workflow:**
1. Scheduling and reminder sequence (appointment to post-visit)
2. Virtual waiting room protocol
3. Technical failure contingency plan
4. Post-visit follow-up automation

Keep all materials at a 6th-grade reading level for patient-facing content. Include accessibility considerations for patients with hearing or vision impairments.

Pro Tips for Healthcare

  • Ask the AI to cite evidence levels (RCT, meta-analysis, expert consensus) when generating clinical recommendations.
  • Specify whether the output is for patient-facing communication or provider-to-provider documentation - the language differs significantly.
  • Always include disclaimers that AI-generated healthcare content must be reviewed by qualified professionals before clinical use.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You need to create patient education handouts that explain a diagnosis or procedure in language patients can actually understand.
  • Your clinic is standardizing SOAP note documentation and you need consistent templates that meet billing and compliance requirements.
  • You are preparing a medical literature review for grand rounds and need a structured synthesis of recent evidence on a clinical topic.

Expected Results

  • Telehealth workflow documents covering patient preparation, provider checklists, and documentation templates.
  • Patient education materials written at the specified reading level with medical terms defined in plain language.
  • Structured clinical documentation (SOAP notes, care plans) following standard medical documentation conventions.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Swap the specialty references with your clinical specialty so documentation follows the right conventions.
  • Include your facility type (hospital, clinic, telehealth) so workflows match your practice setting.
  • Modify the compliance framework references to match your specific regulatory requirements (HIPAA, Joint Commission, state-specific).
