Return and Refund Policy Writer

Create clear, fair return and refund policies that protect your business while building customer trust.

By Arshad Hossain

How to Use

Copy & paste the prompt below into your preferred LLM. Unless a specific AI model is mentioned, you can use whichever you prefer.

The Prompt

You are a business operations and customer experience specialist. Write a return and refund policy for my business.

My business:
- Business type: [ECOMMERCE / SAAS / SERVICE / DIGITAL PRODUCTS / PHYSICAL PRODUCTS]
- Products: [DESCRIBE WHAT YOU SELL]
- Current policy: [DESCRIBE EXISTING POLICY, OR "NONE"]
- Customer expectations: [WHAT DO CUSTOMERS TYPICALLY EXPECT IN YOUR INDUSTRY?]
- Business constraints: [MARGINS, PERISHABILITY, CUSTOMIZATION, DIGITAL DELIVERY]
- Competitor policies: [WHAT DO COMPETITORS OFFER?]

Deliver:
1. **The Policy Document:** Clear, customer-friendly policy covering:
   - Eligibility (what can be returned, what can't, and why)
   - Timeframe (return window with specific day counts)
   - Condition requirements (original packaging, unused, etc.)
   - Process (step-by-step how to initiate a return)
   - Refund method and timeline (original payment, store credit, exchange)
   - Shipping costs (who pays for return shipping?)
   - Exceptions clearly listed (sale items, custom orders, digital products)
2. **FAQ Section:** 10 common questions customers ask about returns, with clear answers
3. **Email Templates:** Approval, denial (with empathy), refund processed, exchange confirmation
4. **Internal Team Guide:** Decision tree for edge cases, authority levels, documentation requirements
5. **Legal Considerations:** Disclaimer language, consumer protection law notes by region
6. **Policy Page Design:** How to display this on your website for maximum clarity and trust

A generous, clear return policy increases sales more than it increases returns.

Why "Return and Refund Policy Writer" Works

This prompt produces reliable output because it leverages success criteria and depth requirements and structured enumeration. Each element gives the AI model additional signal about what quality looks like for this specific task. This means you get empathy-driven response frameworks that de-escalate issues while protecting brand reputation without the trial-and-error that wastes most people's time with AI.

When to Use "Return and Refund Policy Writer"

"Return and Refund Policy Writer" is particularly useful in these situations. If any of these scenarios sound familiar, this prompt will save you significant time.

  • You are setting up a chatbot and need conversation flows that resolve common issues without escalating to a human agent.
  • A product defect is generating a spike in complaints and you need a crisis response template that acknowledges the issue.
  • A customer is threatening to leave and you need a retention script that addresses their frustration while offering a resolution.

What You Will Get from "Return and Refund Policy Writer"

When you use "Return and Refund Policy Writer" with ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini, here is what to expect in the AI output.

  • Escalation decision trees that route issues to the right team based on severity and customer tier.
  • Crisis communication templates that acknowledge issues, provide timelines, and offer interim solutions.
  • Response templates for top complaint categories that feel personal while maintaining brand consistency.

How to Customize "Return and Refund Policy Writer"

Adapt "Return and Refund Policy Writer" to your specific situation by modifying these key areas. The more context you add, the better the results.

  • Modify the response time commitments to reflect your actual SLA targets for each issue severity level.
  • Replace the product name placeholder with your actual product or service for relevant response templates.
  • Adjust the tone to match your brand voice: empathetic and warm, professional and efficient, or casual and friendly.
