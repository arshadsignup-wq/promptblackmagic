SEO Content Brief Generator

Create detailed content briefs that give writers everything they need to produce SEO-optimized content that ranks and converts.

By Arshad Hossain

How to Use

Paste into any LLM with a target keyword. Use the brief to guide your writer or yourself to create content that outperforms competitors.

The Prompt

You are an SEO content strategist who has written briefs that produced 500+ first-page-ranking articles, with a proven process for analyzing search intent and competitor content to create superior content.

[TARGET KEYWORD]: Primary keyword to target
[SECONDARY KEYWORDS]: 3-5 related keywords
[CONTENT TYPE]: Blog post / Guide / Landing page / Product page
[TARGET AUDIENCE]: Who will read this
[BUSINESS GOAL]: What action you want readers to take
[BRAND VOICE]: Tone and style guidelines

Generate a comprehensive content brief:

**1. Keyword and Intent Analysis**
- Primary keyword: search volume, difficulty, intent type
- Secondary keywords with volumes
- LSI (related) keywords to naturally include
- Search intent classification: informational, commercial, transactional, navigational
- Searcher's question: what exactly are they trying to accomplish?

**2. SERP Analysis**
- Top 5 ranking pages: title, URL, word count, content type
- Common themes across top results
- Content gaps in existing top results
- SERP features present and how to target them
- Unique angle that would differentiate your content

**3. Content Outline**
- Recommended title (with keyword, under 60 characters)
- Meta description (with keyword, under 155 characters)
- H1 tag
- Detailed H2/H3 structure with talking points for each section
- Word count target with reasoning
- Introduction framework (hook, context, promise)
- Conclusion framework (summary, CTA)

**4. On-Page SEO Requirements**
- Keyword placement checklist (title, H1, first 100 words, H2s, conclusion)
- Internal linking targets (3-5 pages to link to)
- External linking guidance (authority sources to reference)
- Image requirements with alt text keywords
- Schema markup recommendation
- URL slug suggestion

**5. Content Quality Guidelines**
- E-E-A-T signals to include (experience, expertise, authority, trust)
- Original data, examples, or insights to add
- Multimedia elements (images, charts, videos, infographics)
- Readability targets (grade level, sentence length)
- Formatting best practices (short paragraphs, bullet lists, bold key points)

**6. Competitive Advantage Elements**
- What to include that competitors miss
- Fresh angle or unique perspective
- Better examples or case studies
- More actionable takeaways
- Visual or interactive elements that add value

Why "SEO Content Brief Generator" Works

"SEO Content Brief Generator" applies research-backed prompting principles: structured enumeration and reasoning elicitation. These are the same techniques used by professional prompt engineers to get predictable, high-quality results. The output you receive will be search-intent-matched content with proper heading structure and semantic keyword coverage, ready to use with minimal editing.

Pro Tips for Using "SEO Content Brief Generator"

These content & seo tips will help you get stronger results when using "SEO Content Brief Generator" and similar prompts in this category.

  • Specify the content format you need - listicle, how-to guide, comparison, or pillar page - as each has different structural requirements.
  • Include your target word count and competitor URLs so the AI creates content that can realistically compete in search results.
  • Ask the AI to structure content with proper H2/H3 headings that include semantic variations of your target keyword.

When to Use "SEO Content Brief Generator"

"SEO Content Brief Generator" is particularly useful in these situations. If any of these scenarios sound familiar, this prompt will save you significant time.

  • Your existing content is losing rankings and you need to refresh it with updated information and better structure.
  • Your site has thin pages that Google is ignoring, and you need to expand them with valuable, search-optimized content.
  • You need meta titles and descriptions for 50 pages and want each one to be unique, keyword-rich, and click-worthy.

What You Will Get from "SEO Content Brief Generator"

When you use "SEO Content Brief Generator" with ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini, here is what to expect in the AI output.

  • Internal linking maps that connect new content to existing pages for stronger topical authority.
  • Meta titles and descriptions that balance keyword targeting with click-through rate optimization.
  • Content refresh recommendations that identify outdated sections and suggest updated information.

How to Customize "SEO Content Brief Generator"

Adapt "SEO Content Brief Generator" to your specific situation by modifying these key areas. The more context you add, the better the results.

  • Include URLs of your existing related content so the AI can suggest natural internal linking opportunities.
  • Swap the audience description with your actual reader persona including expertise level and search intent.
  • Adjust the content length to match what currently ranks on page one for your target search query.
