Brand Name Generator

Describe your product, audience, and vibe. Get 20 brand name options with domain availability tips, logo direction, and tagline pairings.

By Arshad Hossain

How to Use

Copy the prompt and fill in your product and brand details.

The Prompt

Generate brand name options for my business.

What it does: [DESCRIBE YOUR PRODUCT/SERVICE]
Target audience: [WHO IS IT FOR]
Brand personality: [MODERN / PLAYFUL / PREMIUM / TECHNICAL / WARM]
Industry: [YOUR INDUSTRY]
Names to avoid sounding like: [COMPETITORS]

Provide 20 name options across these styles:
- 4 invented/coined words (like Spotify, Figma)
- 4 real word combinations (like Airbnb, YouTube)
- 4 descriptive names (like General Electric, Whole Foods)
- 4 metaphorical names (like Amazon, Apple)
- 4 abbreviated or acronym-based (like IBM, HBO)

For each name:
- The name
- Why it works for this brand
- Potential .com domain (or alternative like .io, .co)
- A tagline that pairs with it

Highlight your top 3 recommendations and explain why.

Why "Brand Name Generator" Works

"Brand Name Generator" eliminates the most common reason AI output disappoints - vague instructions. This prompt uses creative divergence and reasoning elicitation to define both what the output should include and how it should be structured. What you get back is original creative directions with enough specificity to execute without additional briefing - production-ready rather than a rough draft that needs heavy reworking.

Pro Tips for Using "Brand Name Generator"

These creative tips will help you get stronger results when using "Brand Name Generator" and similar prompts in this category.

  • Request multiple creative options (5+) and then ask the AI to combine the best elements from each into a final version.
  • Give the AI rich context - character backgrounds, settings, mood, and themes - before asking it to create. Creative output is only as good as the creative brief.
  • Specify constraints to boost creativity - word limits, required elements, or format restrictions often produce more inventive results than total freedom.

When to Use "Brand Name Generator"

"Brand Name Generator" is particularly useful in these situations. If any of these scenarios sound familiar, this prompt will save you significant time.

  • You are developing a content series and need a unifying theme that ties diverse episodes or installments together.
  • Your creative brief is vague and you need to extract a clear direction before presenting concepts to the client.
  • Your project requires visual concepts described in enough detail for a designer or AI image generator to execute.

What You Will Get from "Brand Name Generator"

When you use "Brand Name Generator" with ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini, here is what to expect in the AI output.

  • Multiple creative concepts with unique angles, themes, and execution approaches for client selection.
  • Visual concept descriptions detailed enough for designers or AI image generators to execute accurately.
  • Creative brief expansions that transform vague direction into specific, actionable creative parameters.

How to Customize "Brand Name Generator"

Adapt "Brand Name Generator" to your specific situation by modifying these key areas. The more context you add, the better the results.

  • Add your target audience demographics and psychographics so creative output resonates with the right people.
  • Include your brand guidelines or aesthetic references so the AI maintains visual and tonal consistency.
  • Swap the example themes with your actual campaign message or story premise for relevant ideation.
