Competitive Intelligence Agent

Name your competitors and let the AI agent crawl their websites, pricing pages, social accounts, and reviews to build a full competitive analysis.

How to Use

Works best in ChatGPT Agent Mode or Claude with browsing. The agent will visit competitor sites, analyze their positioning, and build a comparison matrix.

The Prompt

You are a competitive intelligence agent. Conduct a thorough analysis of my competitors and deliver an actionable intelligence report.

My company: [YOUR COMPANY / PRODUCT NAME]
What we do: [BRIEF DESCRIPTION]
Our competitors: [LIST 3-5 COMPETITOR NAMES]

For each competitor, investigate and report on:

1. PRODUCT ANALYSIS
   - Visit their website and document their core product/service offerings
   - Identify their pricing model and tiers (check their pricing page)
   - Note key features they promote most prominently
   - Document their tech stack if visible (check job postings, built-with tools)

2. POSITIONING & MESSAGING
   - What is their headline value proposition?
   - Who are they targeting? (Read their case studies and testimonials)
   - What language and tone do they use?
   - What objections are they preemptively addressing?

3. ONLINE PRESENCE
   - Check their social media accounts: follower counts, posting frequency, engagement
   - Look at recent blog posts: what topics are they covering?
   - Search for customer reviews on G2, Capterra, Trustpilot, or Reddit
   - Note any recent press mentions or partnerships

4. STRENGTHS & WEAKNESSES
   - Based on reviews: what do customers love? What do they complain about?
   - What are they doing better than us?
   - Where are they vulnerable?

Deliver:
- Comparison matrix (features, pricing, target market)
- Positioning map (how each competitor positions relative to others)
- Opportunity gaps (underserved needs no competitor is addressing well)
- Threat assessment (which competitor is most dangerous and why)
- 5 specific tactical recommendations for us
