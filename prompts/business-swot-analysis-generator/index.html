Business SWOT Analysis Generator

Get a comprehensive SWOT analysis for any business, product, or strategic initiative with actionable recommendations based on the findings.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Copy and paste this prompt into your preferred LLM. Fill in the details about your business for a customized analysis.

The Prompt

You are a strategic business consultant conducting a SWOT analysis.

Business: [COMPANY NAME OR DESCRIPTION]
Industry: [YOUR INDUSTRY]
Current stage: [STARTUP / GROWTH / MATURE / TURNAROUND]
Specific context: [WHAT DECISION OR SITUATION PROMPTED THIS ANALYSIS]
Key competitors: [TOP 2-3 COMPETITORS]

Deliver a comprehensive SWOT analysis:

**Strengths (Internal Positives):**
- List 5-7 genuine strengths with specific evidence for each
- Rank them by strategic importance
- Note which strengths are defensible vs. easily copied

**Weaknesses (Internal Negatives):**
- List 5-7 honest weaknesses (do not sugarcoat)
- Classify as fixable (with effort) vs. structural
- Estimate the impact of each on revenue or growth

**Opportunities (External Positives):**
- List 5-7 market opportunities with estimated timeline
- Note which opportunities align with your strengths
- Rank by potential impact and effort required

**Threats (External Negatives):**
- List 5-7 threats with likelihood and severity ratings
- Identify which threats could be existential vs. manageable
- Note early warning signs to monitor

**Strategic Recommendations:**
1. **SO strategies:** How to use strengths to capture opportunities (top 3)
2. **WO strategies:** How to overcome weaknesses to access opportunities (top 3)
3. **ST strategies:** How to use strengths to defend against threats (top 3)
4. **WT strategies:** How to minimize weaknesses and avoid threats (top 3)

**Priority action items:** The 5 most impactful moves to make in the next 90 days, based on this analysis.

Be specific and actionable. Avoid generic business advice that could apply to any company.

Pro Tips for Business

  • When prompting for business strategy, provide context about your industry, company size, and current challenges for actionable advice.
  • Ask the AI to structure its output as a step-by-step action plan rather than general advice - it forces concrete, implementable suggestions.
  • Include your budget constraints or resource limitations in the prompt so the AI recommends realistic solutions.

When to Use This Prompt

  • Your team is stuck on a strategic decision and you need a clear comparison of options with pros, cons, and risk factors.
  • You are entering a new market and need competitive intelligence organized into actionable insights.
  • Your quarterly planning session is tomorrow and you need a prioritized roadmap based on limited resources and tight deadlines.

Expected Results

  • A structured action plan with specific milestones, timelines, and resource requirements.
  • SWOT analysis or competitive framework that maps strengths and threats to strategic decisions.
  • Financial projections or budget breakdowns with realistic assumptions and scenario modeling.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Adjust the company size references to match your actual headcount and revenue range for realistic advice.
  • Swap generic competitor names with your real top three competitors for actionable competitive analysis.
  • Modify the budget parameters to reflect your actual financial constraints so strategies are implementable.
