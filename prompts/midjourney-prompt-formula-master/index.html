Midjourney Prompt Formula Master

Learn the exact formula for writing Midjourney prompts. Get templates, parameter cheat sheets, and 10 ready-to-use prompts for any subject.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into any LLM. Describe what you want to create and get back structured Midjourney prompts with all the right parameters.

The Prompt

You are the world's leading Midjourney prompt engineer. You've generated thousands of images and understand exactly how Midjourney interprets language, parameters, and style references.

What I want to create: [DESCRIBE YOUR VISION]
Style: [PHOTOREALISTIC/ILLUSTRATION/3D RENDER/ABSTRACT/ANIME]
Mood: [DESCRIBE THE FEELING]

First, teach me your Midjourney prompt formula:

1. **The Formula** - Break down the exact structure: [Subject] + [Medium] + [Style] + [Lighting] + [Color] + [Composition] + [Quality boosters] + [Parameters]

2. **Parameter Cheat Sheet** - Explain every useful parameter: --ar, --s, --c, --q, --v, --style, --no, --seed, --tile, --weird. When to use each one and at what values.

3. **Quality Boosters** - List the 20 most powerful keywords that improve output quality (e.g., 'octane render', '8k', 'masterpiece', 'award-winning', etc.) and explain when each works best.

4. **Word Order Matters** - Explain how Midjourney weights words by position and how to use :: for custom weighting.

5. **10 Ready-to-Use Prompts** - Generate 10 complete Midjourney prompts for my subject, each with a different style/approach. Include all parameters. Make them copy-paste ready.

6. **Common Mistakes** - List the top 10 mistakes that produce bad Midjourney results and how to fix each one.

Be specific, technical, and actionable. No fluff.

Pro Tips for Image Generation

  • Reference specific art styles, lighting setups, and color palettes rather than vague descriptors like "beautiful" or "professional" for more consistent results.
  • Specify the aspect ratio and intended use (Instagram post, website hero, print) so the composition works for your platform.
  • Describe the mood with 5-8 specific adjectives rather than generic terms - "warm golden hour glow with soft bokeh" beats "nice lighting" every time.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You need to visualize a concept, mockup, or prototype before investing in professional production.
  • You are exploring different visual styles for a rebrand and want to rapidly test multiple creative directions.
  • You need product photography for your e-commerce store but cannot afford a professional photographer or studio rental.

Expected Results

  • Platform-specific aspect ratios and composition guidelines for each intended use case.
  • Color palette recommendations with hex codes that complement the subject and intended brand aesthetic.
  • Detailed, copy-paste ready prompts for AI image generators with camera settings, lighting specs, and style keywords.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Replace the product description with your actual product name, dimensions, materials, and key visual features.
  • Swap the style preference for your brand aesthetic - minimal, luxury, playful, bold, or organic.
  • Change the platform specification to match where the image will be used (Instagram square, website banner, print ad).
