Hero's Journey Landing Page Template

A versatile template structured around the Hero's Journey to paint a "before" and "after" scenario and drive action with a clear call to action.

By Arshad Hossain

How to Use

Copy & paste the prompt below into your preferred LLM. Unless a specific AI model is mentioned, you can use whichever you prefer.

The Prompt

You are an expert landing page copywriting specialist. Please craft a 750-word landing page for my [high-ticket coaching program] using the Hero's Journey structure. Paint a picture of the "before" state, reveal the solution, and illustrate the triumphant "after." Wrap up with a CTA encouraging readers to book a call or sign up. Make sure to ask me detailed questions about my product/service and target audience so you can complete the task to the best of your ability.

Why "Hero's Journey Landing Page Template" Works

The effectiveness of "Hero's Journey Landing Page Template" comes from its concise structure. By incorporating structured enumeration and role assignment and audience specification, it channels the AI's capabilities toward producing exactly what you need. The end result is buyer-psychology-informed messaging that handles objections and moves deals forward, delivered on the first try rather than after multiple failed attempts.

Pro Tips for Using "Hero's Journey Landing Page Template"

These sales tips will help you get stronger results when using "Hero's Journey Landing Page Template" and similar prompts in this category.

  • Always include your prospect's industry and pain points in the prompt - generic sales messages get ignored, personalized ones get meetings.
  • Specify whether you need cold outreach, follow-up, or closing copy - each stage requires fundamentally different messaging.
  • Include your product pricing tier so the AI can calibrate value propositions appropriate to the deal size.

When to Use "Hero's Journey Landing Page Template"

"Hero's Journey Landing Page Template" is particularly useful in these situations. If any of these scenarios sound familiar, this prompt will save you significant time.

  • You are preparing a proposal for an enterprise client and need a persuasive executive summary that leads with ROI.
  • You are onboarding a new sales rep and need a playbook that covers discovery calls, demos, and closing techniques.
  • Your competitor just launched a cheaper alternative and you need battle cards that highlight your unique advantages.

What You Will Get from "Hero's Journey Landing Page Template"

When you use "Hero's Journey Landing Page Template" with ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini, here is what to expect in the AI output.

  • Discovery call question frameworks that uncover budget, authority, need, and timeline efficiently.
  • Follow-up sequences with varied touchpoints across email, phone, and social for multi-channel outreach.
  • Objection-handling scripts that reframe concerns into buying reasons using specific value evidence.

How to Customize "Hero's Journey Landing Page Template"

Adapt "Hero's Journey Landing Page Template" to your specific situation by modifying these key areas. The more context you add, the better the results.

  • Replace the prospect industry placeholder with your actual target vertical for relevant pain point messaging.
  • Adjust the deal size references to match your pricing tier so the AI calibrates urgency and formality.
  • Modify the follow-up timeline to match your actual sales cycle length: one week for SMB, months for enterprise.
