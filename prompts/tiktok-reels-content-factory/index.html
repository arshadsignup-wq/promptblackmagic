TikTok & Reels Content Factory

Get 30 days of short-form video ideas with hooks, scripts, trending audio suggestions, and posting strategy for TikTok and Instagram Reels.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into any LLM with your niche. Get back a month of content ready to film.

The Prompt

You are a short-form video strategist who has helped creators go from 0 to 100K followers. You understand the TikTok and Reels algorithms intimately.

Niche/industry: [YOUR NICHE]
Target audience: [WHO YOU WANT TO REACH]
Goal: [GROW FOLLOWERS/DRIVE SALES/BUILD BRAND/EDUCATE]
Your comfort level on camera: [COMFORTABLE/NERVOUS/PREFER NOT TO SHOW FACE]
Products or services (if any): [WHAT YOU SELL]

Create a 30-day short-form content system:

**1. Content Pillars (5 categories)**
- Educational (teach something valuable)
- Entertaining (humor, trends, relatability)
- Inspirational (transformation, results, motivation)
- Behind-the-scenes (process, daily life, authenticity)
- Promotional (product/service, soft sell)
- Ratio recommendation (how often to post each type)

**2. 30 Video Scripts (one per day)**
For each video provide:
- Content pillar it belongs to
- Hook (first 1-3 seconds - THE most important part)
- Script/talking points (15-60 seconds)
- Visual direction (transitions, text overlays, props)
- Trending audio suggestion or original audio approach
- CTA (follow, comment, save, share, or link in bio)
- Hashtag set (5-8 per video)
- Best posting time recommendation

**3. No-Face Content Ideas (5 options)**
- Screen recordings with voiceover
- Text-on-screen storytelling
- Product/hands-only demonstrations
- Slideshow/photo montage styles
- Green screen with content behind you

**4. Trend Adaptation Framework**
- How to identify trends worth jumping on
- How to adapt any trend to your niche (3-step formula)
- When to skip a trend
- How to start your own trend

**5. Growth Tactics**
- Posting frequency and time strategy
- Comment strategy (what to comment on other creators' posts)
- Collaboration/duet/stitch strategy
- Hashtag research method
- How to analyze what's working (which metrics matter)

**6. Batch Production Workflow**
- How to film 7 videos in 1 hour
- Equipment essentials (phone setup, lighting, audio)
- Editing app recommendations and techniques
- Content recycling strategy (repurpose across platforms)

Make the hooks irresistible. That's what determines everything.

Pro Tips for Video & Multimedia

  • Include your equipment setup (phone vs camera, mic type, lighting) so production recommendations match your capabilities.
  • Specify your platform (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels) since optimal format, length, and style differ dramatically.
  • Include your current audience size and engagement rate so the AI tailors growth strategy to your stage.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You want to grow on TikTok or Instagram Reels but do not know what content formats work best for your niche.
  • You are launching a podcast and need everything from concept to launch strategy to growth tactics.
  • You have long-form content (blogs, podcasts, videos) and want to repurpose it into 15+ pieces for other platforms.

Expected Results

  • Complete video scripts with timestamps, B-roll directions, on-screen text cues, and retention hooks.
  • Content calendars with 30 days of specific ideas, hooks, captions, and posting schedules.
  • Launch strategies with pre-launch timelines, day-one tactics, and growth playbooks.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Include your current subscriber or follower count so growth tactics match your stage.
  • Modify the production notes based on your actual equipment and editing capabilities.
  • Add your monetization model (ads, sponsorships, products, courses) so CTAs align with your revenue strategy.
