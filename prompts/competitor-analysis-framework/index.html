Competitor Analysis Framework

Name your competitors and get a structured comparison covering pricing, features, positioning, strengths, weaknesses, and where you can win.

How to Use

Copy the prompt and list your competitors along with your own product details.

The Prompt

Create a competitive analysis for my business.

My product: [NAME AND WHAT IT DOES]
Competitors to analyze: [LIST 3-5 COMPETITORS]

For each competitor, analyze:
1. Positioning (who they target and how they describe themselves)
2. Pricing model and price points
3. Key features (what they offer that I should know about)
4. Strengths (what they do better than others)
5. Weaknesses (where they fall short)
6. Customer sentiment (what reviews say)

Then provide:
- A comparison matrix (table format)
- Gaps in the market none of them are filling
- My strongest differentiation opportunities
- Specific messaging angles to position against each competitor
- Features I should prioritize building based on competitive gaps
