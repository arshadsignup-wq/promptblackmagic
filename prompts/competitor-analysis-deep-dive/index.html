Competitor Analysis Deep Dive

Name your competitors and your product. Get a strategic analysis with positioning gaps, feature comparisons, and opportunities to differentiate.

How to Use

List your competitors and what you know about them. Use the analysis to refine your positioning and strategy.

The Prompt

Conduct a thorough competitive analysis for my business.

My company/product: [WHAT YOU SELL AND WHO YOU SELL TO]
My top competitors:
1. [COMPETITOR 1 NAME AND WHAT THEY DO]
2. [COMPETITOR 2 NAME AND WHAT THEY DO]
3. [COMPETITOR 3 NAME AND WHAT THEY DO]

My current pricing: [YOUR PRICE POINT]
My main differentiator: [WHAT YOU THINK MAKES YOU DIFFERENT]

Analyze:

1. Feature comparison matrix:
   - Table comparing key features across all competitors and me
   - For each feature: who does it best and why
   - Gaps: features they all have that I lack (urgent to address)
   - Opportunities: features nobody offers yet that I could own

2. Positioning analysis:
   - How each competitor positions themselves (their messaging, tagline, target audience)
   - Where there is white space in the market nobody is claiming
   - A 2x2 positioning map with suggested axes relevant to my industry

3. Pricing analysis:
   - Compare pricing models (freemium, subscription, one-time, usage-based)
   - Where am I on the price spectrum and is that the right position?
   - Pricing strategy recommendations

4. Strengths to attack: Where each competitor is weakest (based on reviews, common complaints)

5. Threats to defend: Where I am most vulnerable

6. Strategic recommendations: Top 5 actions I should take based on this analysis, prioritized by impact and effort

7. Messaging angles: 3 marketing messages that highlight where I win vs. each competitor
