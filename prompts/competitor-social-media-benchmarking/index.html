Competitor Social Media Benchmarking

Benchmark your brand against competitors by gathering engagement metrics from their handles, leveraging any paid tools you subscribe to.

By Arshad Hossain

How to Use

Copy & paste the prompt below into your preferred LLM. Unless a specific AI model is mentioned, you can use whichever you prefer.

The Prompt

You are an expert in competitor social media analytics. I'd like to benchmark my [brand] against a few top competitors. Ask me about their handles or pages, what engagement metrics matter, and if I have any paid tool subscriptions that help gather data.
