Social Media Carousel Template Designer

Define your topic and brand colors, then get AI prompts for a complete carousel post design with cover slide, content slides, and CTA slide.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Use with Midjourney or DALL-E 3 to generate slide designs, then recreate in Canva or Figma with your actual text.

The Prompt

You are a social media designer who creates high-engagement carousel posts for influencers and brands with millions of followers.

Topic: [CAROUSEL TOPIC/TITLE]
Platform: [INSTAGRAM/LINKEDIN/TIKTOK PHOTO MODE]
Brand colors: [PRIMARY COLOR, SECONDARY COLOR, ACCENT]
Visual style: [CLEAN & PROFESSIONAL/BOLD & COLORFUL/MINIMAL & ELEGANT/PLAYFUL & FUN]
Number of slides: [5/7/10]

Design a complete carousel prompt set:

**1. Cover Slide (Slide 1 - The Hook)**
- Bold headline that stops the scroll
- Eye-catching visual element or icon
- Brand colors prominent
- Clear topic preview without giving everything away
- Aspect ratio: 1:1 (Instagram) or 4:5 (LinkedIn)

**2. Content Slides (Slides 2-8)**
- Consistent layout template across all content slides
- Hierarchy: slide number, subheading, body text zone, visual element
- Alternating visual patterns (text-heavy / image-heavy / mixed)
- Progressive reveal of information
- Each slide ends with a reason to swipe

**3. Data / Stats Slide**
- Infographic-style data visualization
- Large numbers with context
- Chart or graph placeholder design
- Source citation area

**4. Quote / Highlight Slide**
- Pull quote in large typography
- Subtle background pattern or gradient
- Author/source attribution
- Shareable as standalone image

**5. CTA Slide (Final Slide)**
- Clear call-to-action
- Profile tag / handle placement
- Save, share, follow prompts
- Contact or link-in-bio direction
- Cohesive ending that matches cover slide energy

**6. Story/Reel Preview Crop**
- Vertical crop version of cover slide for story promotion
- "New Post" or "Swipe" overlay design

For each slide: write a complete image generation prompt specifying layout, typography style, color usage, spacing, and decorative elements. Include prompts for both light mode and dark mode versions.

Pro Tips for Image Generation

  • Always specify the exact camera, lens, and aperture in your prompt - AI image generators produce dramatically better results with technical photography terms.
  • Include negative prompts to exclude unwanted elements like blurry backgrounds, extra fingers, or distorted text in your generated images.
  • Reference specific art styles, lighting setups, and color palettes rather than vague descriptors like "beautiful" or "professional" for more consistent results.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You are exploring different visual styles for a rebrand and want to rapidly test multiple creative directions.
  • You need product photography for your e-commerce store but cannot afford a professional photographer or studio rental.
  • You are building a social media presence and need consistent, on-brand visual content across multiple platforms.

Expected Results

  • Multiple variations of the same concept covering different styles, angles, and moods for A/B testing.
  • Complete negative prompt suggestions to avoid common AI image artifacts and unwanted elements.
  • Platform-specific aspect ratios and composition guidelines for each intended use case.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Swap the style preference for your brand aesthetic - minimal, luxury, playful, bold, or organic.
  • Change the platform specification to match where the image will be used (Instagram square, website banner, print ad).
  • Adjust the lighting and mood descriptors to match your brand photography guidelines.
