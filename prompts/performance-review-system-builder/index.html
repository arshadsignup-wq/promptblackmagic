Performance Review System Builder

Get a complete performance review framework with self-assessment templates, manager evaluation forms, and calibration guidelines.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into any LLM with your company details. Use the output to build or improve your review process.

The Prompt

You are an HR strategy consultant who has designed performance management systems for companies ranging from 20 to 20,000 employees.

Company size: [NUMBER OF EMPLOYEES]
Review frequency: [ANNUAL/SEMI-ANNUAL/QUARTERLY]
Current challenges: [WHAT'S NOT WORKING WITH CURRENT REVIEWS]
Company values: [LIST 3-5 VALUES]
Industry: [YOUR INDUSTRY]

Design a complete performance review system:

**1. Review Framework**
- Rating scale design (recommend a scale and defend why)
- Performance dimensions (results vs behaviors vs growth)
- Weighting between dimensions
- How to handle different role types (IC vs manager vs exec)

**2. Self-Assessment Template**
- 8-10 reflection questions that produce useful answers
- Achievement documentation format
- Growth areas identification (without making it feel punitive)
- Career aspiration section

**3. Manager Assessment Template**
- Rating criteria with behavioral anchors for each level
- Strength identification framework
- Development area framing (constructive, specific, actionable)
- Potential assessment (ready now, ready in 1-2 years, growth in role)

**4. Peer Feedback Collection**
- 5 peer feedback questions (structured, not open-ended rambling)
- How to select reviewers fairly
- Anonymity guidelines
- How to synthesize peer input

**5. Calibration Process**
- Manager calibration session structure
- Rating distribution guidelines (forced curve vs flexible)
- Bias identification checklist
- Edge case decision framework

**6. Review Conversation Guide**
- Meeting structure (time allocation per section)
- Opening and closing scripts
- How to deliver tough feedback
- How to discuss compensation (or separate it)
- Goal-setting for next period

**7. Compensation Integration**
- How to tie reviews to raises/bonuses
- Merit matrix design
- Communication templates for different outcomes

Include all templates, scripts, and forms ready to implement.

Pro Tips for HR & People Management

  • Include relevant employment laws and compliance requirements for your jurisdiction to avoid legally risky recommendations.
  • Request templates with fill-in-the-blank sections rather than finished documents - every company needs to customize HR materials.
  • Specify whether the output is for the employee or the manager perspective since the framing differs significantly.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You are preparing for performance review season and need a fair, consistent evaluation framework.
  • Employee turnover is higher than you would like and you need a data-driven retention strategy.
  • You are conducting interviews and want to eliminate bias with structured scorecards and behavioral questions.

Expected Results

  • Data-driven frameworks with metrics, benchmarks, and ROI calculations for HR initiatives.
  • Bias-mitigation checklists and compliance considerations integrated into every process.
  • Ready-to-use templates with specific questions, scoring criteria, and evaluation frameworks.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Modify the compensation and benefits references to match what your organization actually offers.
  • Add your existing HR tools and systems (ATS, HRIS, performance platform) for integrated recommendations.
  • Replace the role title with your actual position including level, department, and reporting structure.
