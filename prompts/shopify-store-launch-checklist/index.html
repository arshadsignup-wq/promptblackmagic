Shopify Store Launch Checklist

Get a complete pre-launch checklist for your Shopify store covering product setup, payments, shipping, SEO, and go-live preparations.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into any LLM with your store details. Work through the checklist before launching.

The Prompt

You are a Shopify expert who has launched 200+ successful stores. You know every setting, app, and optimization that matters.

Store name: [STORE NAME]
Products: [WHAT YOU SELL - PHYSICAL/DIGITAL]
Number of products: [HOW MANY AT LAUNCH]
Target market: [COUNTRY/COUNTRIES]
Price range: [LOW/MID/HIGH-END]
Shipping model: [SELF-FULFILLED/DROPSHIP/3PL]

Create a comprehensive launch checklist:

**1. Store Foundation**
- Theme selection criteria and top 3 recommendations
- Essential pages to create (with content guidelines for each)
- Navigation structure
- Domain and email setup
- Legal pages (privacy, terms, refund policy) - what each must include

**2. Product Setup**
- Product title optimization formula
- Description template (features, benefits, specs)
- Image requirements (dimensions, count, types of shots)
- Variant and option organization
- Collection/category strategy
- Inventory tracking setup
- SEO fields for each product

**3. Payment & Checkout**
- Payment gateway setup checklist
- Express checkout configuration (Shop Pay, Apple Pay, Google Pay)
- Checkout customization priorities
- Tax configuration by market
- Currency settings for international

**4. Shipping Configuration**
- Shipping zone setup
- Rate strategy (free threshold, flat rate, calculated)
- Shipping profile configuration
- Packaging specifications
- Tracking and notification setup

**5. Essential Apps (10 max)**
- Email marketing
- Reviews
- SEO
- Analytics
- Upsell/cross-sell
- For each: specific app recommendation with free vs paid analysis

**6. Pre-Launch Marketing**
- 'Coming soon' page setup
- Email list building pre-launch
- Social media account setup
- Launch day promotion plan
- Influencer seeding strategy

**7. Testing Checklist**
- Place test order (full flow)
- Mobile responsiveness check
- Page speed test
- Broken link check
- Email notification review
- Payment processing verification

**8. Go-Live Day**
- Hour-by-hour launch day plan
- Monitoring priorities
- Common Day 1 issues and fixes

Mark each item as [CRITICAL], [IMPORTANT], or [NICE-TO-HAVE].

Pro Tips for E-Commerce

  • Ask for A/B test ideas alongside each recommendation so you can validate changes before committing.
  • Include your shipping model and return policy details since they significantly impact conversion and copy strategy.
  • Request mobile-specific optimizations separately - mobile e-commerce has different UX requirements than desktop.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You are launching a Shopify store and need a comprehensive checklist to avoid costly mistakes at launch.
  • Your product descriptions are not converting and you need persuasive copy optimized for each sales channel.
  • Your cart abandonment rate is above 70% and you need a multi-channel recovery system to recapture lost sales.

Expected Results

  • Conversion-focused page layouts with persuasion elements, trust signals, and CTA positioning.
  • A/B test recommendations with specific variables to test and success metrics to track.
  • Platform-specific product copy optimized for Shopify, Amazon, and social commerce channels.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Adjust the target customer profile to match your actual buyer demographics and purchase behavior.
  • Include your shipping model, return policy, and payment options for accurate checkout optimization.
  • Modify the pricing and discount strategy to match your margins and competitive positioning.
