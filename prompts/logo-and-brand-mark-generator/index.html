Logo and Brand Mark Generator

Input your brand name and values to get detailed AI prompts for generating professional logos, icons, and brand marks.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into any LLM with your brand details filled in. Use the output prompts in Midjourney, DALL-E, or Ideogram.

The Prompt

You are a senior brand identity designer with 20 years of experience creating logos for startups and global brands alike.

Brand name: [BRAND NAME]
Industry: [INDUSTRY]
Brand values: [3-5 CORE VALUES]
Style preference: [WORDMARK/ICON/COMBINATION/EMBLEM/ABSTRACT]
Color preference: [COLORS OR 'SUGGEST FOR ME']

Generate 8 distinct AI image prompts for logo concepts:

1. **Minimal Wordmark** - Clean typography, modern sans-serif, no icon
2. **Icon + Wordmark** - Simple geometric icon paired with type
3. **Abstract Symbol** - Unique abstract mark that captures brand essence
4. **Lettermark/Monogram** - Initials designed as a unified mark
5. **Emblem Style** - Badge or crest format, premium feel
6. **Negative Space Concept** - Clever use of hidden shapes within letters
7. **Geometric Minimalist** - Based on circles, squares, golden ratio
8. **Hand-Drawn/Organic** - Artisan, human-touch feel

For each prompt specify:
- Exact style descriptors (flat design, 3D, gradient, line art)
- Background (white, dark, transparent)
- Typography style if applicable
- Mandatory keywords: vector, scalable, professional, high contrast
- Negative prompts: avoid photorealistic, avoid complex details, avoid text errors

Also provide guidance on which logo style works best for: app icons, business cards, social media avatars, and large signage.

Pro Tips for Image Generation

  • Specify the aspect ratio and intended use (Instagram post, website hero, print) so the composition works for your platform.
  • Describe the mood with 5-8 specific adjectives rather than generic terms - "warm golden hour glow with soft bokeh" beats "nice lighting" every time.
  • Always specify the exact camera, lens, and aperture in your prompt - AI image generators produce dramatically better results with technical photography terms.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You need product photography for your e-commerce store but cannot afford a professional photographer or studio rental.
  • You are building a social media presence and need consistent, on-brand visual content across multiple platforms.
  • You want to create unique art or illustrations for a project without hiring a graphic designer.

Expected Results

  • Color palette recommendations with hex codes that complement the subject and intended brand aesthetic.
  • Detailed, copy-paste ready prompts for AI image generators with camera settings, lighting specs, and style keywords.
  • Multiple variations of the same concept covering different styles, angles, and moods for A/B testing.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Change the platform specification to match where the image will be used (Instagram square, website banner, print ad).
  • Adjust the lighting and mood descriptors to match your brand photography guidelines.
  • Add your brand colors as hex codes so the AI can incorporate them into backgrounds and styling.
