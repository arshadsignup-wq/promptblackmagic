Employee Onboarding Program Builder

Define the role and company culture, then get a complete 90-day onboarding program with checklists, training schedules, and milestone reviews.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into any LLM with your company and role details. Use the output as your onboarding framework for new hires.

The Prompt

You are a people operations leader who has built onboarding programs that reduce new hire time-to-productivity by 40% and increase 1-year retention by 25%.

Role being onboarded: [JOB TITLE AND DEPARTMENT]
Company size: [STARTUP/SMB/ENTERPRISE]
Work model: [IN-OFFICE/REMOTE/HYBRID]
Team size: [HOW MANY PEOPLE ON THE TEAM]
Biggest onboarding gap: [WHAT'S CURRENTLY MISSING OR BROKEN]

Build a complete 90-day onboarding program:

**Pre-Day 1 (Offer to Start)**
- Welcome email template (tone, content, key info)
- IT setup checklist (hardware, software, accounts, access)
- Pre-reading materials (company handbook, org chart, team wiki)
- Buddy/mentor assignment process
- First week calendar pre-populated
- Welcome package contents

**Week 1: Orientation & Foundation**
- Day 1 schedule (hour by hour)
- HR paperwork and compliance training
- Company culture presentation outline
- Team introductions format
- Tools and systems walkthrough
- First meaningful task (small win on Day 1)
- End of Week 1 check-in questions

**Weeks 2-4: Learning & Contributing**
- Role-specific training curriculum
- Shadow sessions with team members (who, when, what to observe)
- First independent project assignment
- 1:1 meeting cadence with manager (agenda template)
- Cross-functional introductions schedule
- 30-day check-in: questions and assessment criteria

**Weeks 5-8: Building & Growing**
- Increased responsibility milestones
- Feedback collection (from new hire AND about new hire)
- Goal setting for remainder of first quarter
- Team process deep-dives
- 60-day check-in: performance discussion template

**Weeks 9-12: Owning & Thriving**
- Full role ownership expectations
- 90-day review: formal assessment criteria and template
- Development plan for months 4-12
- Onboarding feedback survey (improve the process)
- Graduation from onboarding: celebration or recognition

**Manager's Onboarding Toolkit**
- Week-by-week manager action items
- Conversation starters for 1:1s
- Red flags to watch for
- When to escalate concerns to HR
- Templates: 30/60/90 review forms

Provide specific content for each item, not just category names. Include actual email templates, meeting agendas, and review form questions.

Pro Tips for HR & People Management

  • Specify whether the output is for the employee or the manager perspective since the framing differs significantly.
  • Specify the company size, industry, and work arrangement (remote/hybrid/on-site) since HR practices vary dramatically by context.
  • Ask the AI to include specific behavioral examples and scoring rubrics rather than generic competency descriptions.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You are conducting interviews and want to eliminate bias with structured scorecards and behavioral questions.
  • You want to build an employer brand that attracts top talent without relying solely on recruiters.
  • You are hiring for a critical role and your job postings are getting low-quality applicants or very few responses.

Expected Results

  • Ready-to-use templates with specific questions, scoring criteria, and evaluation frameworks.
  • Day-by-day or week-by-week schedules with activities, milestones, and check-in agendas.
  • Communication scripts for difficult conversations including feedback delivery and termination discussions.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Replace the role title with your actual position including level, department, and reporting structure.
  • Swap the company size and industry references with your specific organizational context.
  • Adjust the competency frameworks to match your company values and the skills most critical for the role.
