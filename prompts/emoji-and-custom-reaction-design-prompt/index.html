Emoji and Custom Reaction Design Prompt

Define your community or brand and get AI prompts for custom emoji sets, reaction stickers, and animated emote designs for Discord, Slack, or Twitch.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Use with Midjourney or DALL-E 3. Output as 128x128 or 512x512 PNGs with transparent backgrounds for platform upload.

The Prompt

You are an emote designer who creates custom emoji sets for Twitch streamers with 50K+ subscribers, Discord communities, and Slack workspaces.

Community/brand: [GAMING CHANNEL/COMPANY SLACK/DISCORD SERVER/TWITCH CHANNEL]
Mascot or character: [DESCRIBE THE CHARACTER, OR 'CREATE ONE']
Style: [CHIBI/PIXEL ART/FLAT DESIGN/CARTOON/REALISTIC/PEPE-STYLE]
Platform: [DISCORD/SLACK/TWITCH/CUSTOM APP]
Color theme: [BRAND COLORS OR PREFERRED PALETTE]

Generate a complete emoji set (20 designs):

**1. Basic Emotions (6 emotes)**
- Happy/laughing
- Sad/crying
- Angry/frustrated
- Love/heart eyes
- Surprised/shocked
- Thinking/hmm

**2. Reactions (6 emotes)**
- Thumbs up / approval
- Thumbs down / disapproval
- Clapping / celebration
- Eyes / looking / interesting
- Fire / that's hot / great
- Brain / big brain / smart

**3. Status / Activity (4 emotes)**
- Working / typing / busy
- AFK / sleeping / away
- Eating / snack break
- In a meeting / do not disturb

**4. Community-Specific (4 emotes)**
- Inside joke or community catchphrase
- Victory / win / achievement
- Fail / L / defeat
- Hype / excited / let's go

**Design Specifications:**
- Each emote works at 28x28px (Discord inline size) AND 128x128px (selection menu)
- Transparent background
- Bold outlines for readability at small sizes
- Limited color palette (6-8 colors max) for consistency
- Expression readable without zooming in
- No fine details that disappear at small scale

For each emote: write a prompt specifying the exact expression, pose, and any props. Include transparent background instruction, size considerations, and style consistency keywords. Emphasize readability at tiny sizes.

Pro Tips for Image Generation

  • Reference specific art styles, lighting setups, and color palettes rather than vague descriptors like "beautiful" or "professional" for more consistent results.
  • Specify the aspect ratio and intended use (Instagram post, website hero, print) so the composition works for your platform.
  • Describe the mood with 5-8 specific adjectives rather than generic terms - "warm golden hour glow with soft bokeh" beats "nice lighting" every time.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You want to create unique art or illustrations for a project without hiring a graphic designer.
  • You are designing marketing materials and need eye-catching visuals that match a specific brand aesthetic.
  • You need to visualize a concept, mockup, or prototype before investing in professional production.

Expected Results

  • Platform-specific aspect ratios and composition guidelines for each intended use case.
  • Color palette recommendations with hex codes that complement the subject and intended brand aesthetic.
  • Detailed, copy-paste ready prompts for AI image generators with camera settings, lighting specs, and style keywords.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Add your brand colors as hex codes so the AI can incorporate them into backgrounds and styling.
  • Modify the camera and lens specs to match the look you want - wider for environmental, tighter for product detail.
  • Replace the product description with your actual product name, dimensions, materials, and key visual features.
