Concept Art Environment Design

Describe a fictional world and get detailed prompts for concept art environments - from wide establishing shots to detailed location designs.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Use with Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, or DALL-E 3. Customize the world setting and mood in brackets.

The Prompt

You are a concept artist who has designed environments for AAA video games and Hollywood film productions.

World setting: [HIGH FANTASY/CYBERPUNK/POST-APOCALYPTIC/STEAMPUNK/UNDERWATER/SPACE COLONY]
Time period: [ANCIENT/MEDIEVAL/NEAR FUTURE/FAR FUTURE/ALTERNATE HISTORY]
Mood: [EPIC & GRAND/EERIE & MYSTERIOUS/LUSH & PEACEFUL/GRITTY & DANGEROUS/WHIMSICAL]
Key visual theme: [NATURE RECLAIMING TECH/VERTICAL MEGACITY/FROZEN WASTELAND/BIOLUMINESCENT/DESERT OASIS]

Create 6 environment concept art prompts:

**1. Wide Establishing Shot**
- Massive scale, tiny figures for reference
- Full environment visible: sky, horizon, key landmarks
- Dramatic lighting setting the mood
- Color key establishing the palette for the entire world
- Painterly, concept art style (not photorealistic)

**2. Key Location - Exterior**
- The most important building or structure in the world
- Architectural details reflecting the culture
- Environmental storytelling (weathering, usage marks, modifications)
- People interacting with the space
- Multiple material types visible

**3. Key Location - Interior**
- Inside the most significant interior space
- Light sources (natural, artificial, magical) creating atmosphere
- Props and details that tell stories about inhabitants
- Scale reference through furniture and doorways
- Atmosphere (dust motes, fog, colored light)

**4. Dangerous / Conflict Zone**
- Area showing tension, danger, or recent conflict
- Environmental hazards visible
- Dramatic lighting (fire, emergency lights, storm)
- Destruction or transformation of familiar elements
- Narrative tension through visual design

**5. Hidden / Secret Location**
- Discovery moment - viewer finding something unexpected
- Contrast with the main world (different colors, lighting, materials)
- Ancient or forbidden feel
- Small details rewarding close inspection
- Sense of wonder and mystery

**6. Thumbnail Composition Studies (4 variants)**
- Same location, 4 different compositions
- Varying camera angles, lighting conditions, weather
- Small, rough, focusing on value and shape design
- Black and white or limited palette
- Each telling a different story about the same space

For each prompt: include art style references (artists, games, films), color palette descriptions, atmosphere keywords, and composition notes. Write as standalone prompts ready for AI image generation.

Pro Tips for Image Generation

  • Include negative prompts to exclude unwanted elements like blurry backgrounds, extra fingers, or distorted text in your generated images.
  • Reference specific art styles, lighting setups, and color palettes rather than vague descriptors like "beautiful" or "professional" for more consistent results.
  • Specify the aspect ratio and intended use (Instagram post, website hero, print) so the composition works for your platform.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You need product photography for your e-commerce store but cannot afford a professional photographer or studio rental.
  • You are building a social media presence and need consistent, on-brand visual content across multiple platforms.
  • You want to create unique art or illustrations for a project without hiring a graphic designer.

Expected Results

  • Complete negative prompt suggestions to avoid common AI image artifacts and unwanted elements.
  • Platform-specific aspect ratios and composition guidelines for each intended use case.
  • Color palette recommendations with hex codes that complement the subject and intended brand aesthetic.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Change the platform specification to match where the image will be used (Instagram square, website banner, print ad).
  • Adjust the lighting and mood descriptors to match your brand photography guidelines.
  • Add your brand colors as hex codes so the AI can incorporate them into backgrounds and styling.
