Product Description Copy Engine

Input your product specs and get conversion-optimized descriptions for marketplace listings, your website, social ads, and email campaigns.

By Arshad Hossain

How to Use

Paste into any LLM with your product details. Get multi-channel copy that sells benefits, not just features.

The Prompt

You are a DTC copywriter who has written product descriptions that generated $50M+ in sales across Shopify, Amazon, and social commerce channels.

Product: [PRODUCT NAME AND TYPE]
Key features: [LIST 5-7 FEATURES AND SPECS]
Target customer: [WHO BUYS THIS AND WHY]
Price point: [PRICE AND VALUE JUSTIFICATION]
Competitor products: [WHAT ALTERNATIVES EXIST]

Write conversion-optimized copy for every channel:

**1. Website Product Page**
- Headline (benefit-focused, not feature-focused)
- Subheadline (addresses the main objection)
- Opening paragraph (hook with the problem and solution, 50 words)
- Feature-benefit bullets (6-8 bullets, each pairing a feature with its benefit)
- Social proof section (how to present reviews, ratings, user count)
- FAQ section (5 common questions with answers that sell)
- Urgency/scarcity element (if applicable and honest)

**2. Amazon Listing**
- Title (optimized for search, under 200 characters, keyword-rich)
- Bullet points (5, each starting with a CAPITALIZED benefit phrase)
- Product description / A+ Content outline (sections, images, comparison chart)
- Backend keywords (search terms not in visible listing)
- Category and attribute optimization

**3. Social Media Ad Copy (3 variations)**
- Hook-first ad (pattern interrupt in first line)
- Problem-solution ad (agitate the problem, present the fix)
- Social proof ad (lead with customer results)
- Each: primary text, headline, description, CTA

**4. Email Marketing Copy**
- Product launch email (subject line + body)
- Abandoned browse email (subject line + body)
- Review request email (subject line + body)
- Restock reminder email (subject line + body)

**5. Short-Form Copy**
- Instagram caption (engaging, with CTA)
- TikTok script (15-30 seconds, hook in first 3 seconds)
- Pinterest pin description (SEO-optimized)
- Google Shopping feed title and description

**6. SEO Product Content**
- Meta title (60 characters, keyword + brand)
- Meta description (155 characters, benefit + CTA)
- Structured data key fields
- Long-tail keyword targets

All copy must be: benefit-first, specific (not vague), scannable, and ending with a clear CTA. No hype or unverifiable claims.

Why "Product Description Copy Engine" Works

The effectiveness of "Product Description Copy Engine" comes from its in-depth structure. By incorporating sequential task breakdown and output formatting and audience specification, it channels the AI's capabilities toward producing exactly what you need. Your output will be conversion-tested product content optimized for your specific platform and buyer journey stage - the difference between useful AI assistance and a response you immediately delete.

Pro Tips for Using "Product Description Copy Engine"

These e-commerce tips will help you get stronger results when using "Product Description Copy Engine" and similar prompts in this category.

  • Specify your platform (Shopify, WooCommerce, custom) since optimization tactics differ by platform capabilities.
  • Include your shipping model and return policy details since they significantly impact conversion and copy strategy.
  • Include your average order value and current conversion rate so the AI can prioritize high-impact recommendations.

When to Use "Product Description Copy Engine"

"Product Description Copy Engine" is particularly useful in these situations. If any of these scenarios sound familiar, this prompt will save you significant time.

  • You want to increase average order value and need cross-sell and upsell strategies for your product pages.
  • You are launching a Shopify store and need a comprehensive checklist to avoid costly mistakes at launch.
  • Your cart abandonment rate is above 70% and you need a multi-channel recovery system to recapture lost sales.

What You Will Get from "Product Description Copy Engine"

When you use "Product Description Copy Engine" with ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini, here is what to expect in the AI output.

  • Conversion-focused page layouts with persuasion elements, trust signals, and CTA positioning.
  • Platform-specific product copy optimized for Shopify, Amazon, and social commerce channels.
  • Pre-launch checklists with priority rankings (critical, important, nice-to-have) for each item.

How to Customize "Product Description Copy Engine"

Adapt "Product Description Copy Engine" to your specific situation by modifying these key areas. The more context you add, the better the results.

  • Replace the product details with your actual product name, specs, price, and unique selling points.
  • Adjust the target customer profile to match your actual buyer demographics and purchase behavior.
  • Modify the pricing and discount strategy to match your margins and competitive positioning.
