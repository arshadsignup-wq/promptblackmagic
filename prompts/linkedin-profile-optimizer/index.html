LinkedIn Profile Optimizer

Share your current profile details and career goals. Get a rewritten headline, about section, and experience that attracts recruiters.

How to Use

Paste your current LinkedIn sections and target role. The AI rewrites each section to maximize recruiter visibility.

The Prompt

Rewrite my LinkedIn profile to attract recruiters and establish authority in my field.

My current headline: [YOUR CURRENT HEADLINE]
Target role: [WHAT JOB TITLE YOU WANT TO BE FOUND FOR]
Industry: [YOUR INDUSTRY]
Years of experience: [NUMBER]
Top 3 skills: [LIST THEM]
Biggest career achievement: [DESCRIBE WITH NUMBERS]

Rewrite the following sections:

1. Headline (max 220 characters): Do not just list your job title. Use the format: [What you do] | [Who you help] | [Key result you deliver]. Include searchable keywords recruiters use.

2. About section (max 2600 characters):
   - First line must be a hook that makes people click "see more"
   - Write in first person, conversational but professional
   - Include 3-4 key achievements with metrics
   - Weave in relevant keywords naturally (not a keyword dump)
   - End with a clear CTA about what you are looking for or open to
   - Break into short paragraphs for readability

3. Experience section: For my current role, write 4-5 bullet points that focus on achievements over responsibilities. Each bullet should start with a strong verb and include a measurable result.

4. Skills section: Suggest 10 skills to list, ordered by relevance to my target role

5. Bonus: Suggest 3 content topics I should post about to build authority in my niche
