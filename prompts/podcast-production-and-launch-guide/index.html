Podcast Production and Launch Guide

Define your podcast concept and get a complete production guide - from equipment setup to episode structure to distribution and growth strategy.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into any LLM. Use the output to launch a professional-quality podcast on any budget.

The Prompt

You are a podcast producer who has launched 30+ podcasts, several of which reached the top 50 in their category on Apple Podcasts within the first month.

Podcast concept: [TOPIC AND FORMAT]
Format: [SOLO/INTERVIEW/CO-HOSTED/NARRATIVE/PANEL]
Target listener: [WHO AND WHY THEY'D LISTEN]
Episode length target: [15 MIN/30 MIN/45 MIN/60+ MIN]
Budget: [MINIMAL/MODERATE/PROFESSIONAL]

Create a complete podcast production guide:

**1. Pre-Launch Setup**
- Equipment recommendations by budget:
  - Minimal ($50): USB mic, free editing software, phone recording
  - Moderate ($200-500): XLR mic, interface, headphones, acoustic treatment
  - Professional ($1000+): multi-mic setup, mixer, sound treatment, remote recording
- Software: recording (Riverside, Zencastr, Audacity), editing (Descript, Logic, GarageBand)
- Hosting platform comparison (Buzzsprout, Transistor, Anchor, Podbean)
- Cover art specifications (3000x3000px, readable at small size)
- Podcast name validation (search existing podcasts, domain check)

**2. Episode Structure Template**
- Cold open / teaser (15-30 seconds of the best moment)
- Intro music and voiceover (keep under 30 seconds)
- Welcome and episode overview
- Segment 1 (main content or guest introduction)
- Midroll break point (ad or promo placement)
- Segment 2 (deep dive or interview core)
- Segment 3 (actionable takeaways or audience Q&A)
- Outro (CTA, next episode tease, sign-off)
- End music

**3. Interview Framework (if applicable)**
- Guest research template (background, recent work, unique angles)
- Booking email template
- Pre-interview questionnaire
- Interview question structure:
  - Opening: warm-up, rapport builder
  - Core: 5-7 questions progressing from accessible to deep
  - Lightning round or signature segment
  - Closing: plugs, recommendations, final wisdom
- Follow-up and thank you process

**4. Production Workflow**
- Pre-production: outline, research, guest prep (1-2 hours)
- Recording: setup, levels check, recording (1-1.5x episode length)
- Post-production: editing, mixing, mastering (1-3 hours depending on format)
- Show notes: timestamps, links, summary, transcription
- Publishing: upload, metadata, scheduling
- Promotion: social posts, email, cross-promotion

**5. Launch Strategy (First 10 Episodes)**
- Record 3-5 episodes before launching (publish simultaneously)
- Apple Podcasts category selection (primary and secondary)
- Launch week promotion plan (friends, social, email list, cross-pods)
- Review generation strategy (how to ask, when to ask)
- Submit to all directories (Apple, Spotify, Google, Amazon, Pocket Casts)

**6. Growth Tactics**
- Podcast SEO (episode titles, descriptions, transcripts)
- Guest leverage (guests share with their audience)
- Cross-promotion with complementary podcasts
- Audiogram creation for social media
- Newsletter and blog companion content
- Joining podcast networks (when and which)
- Paid promotion (when it makes sense)

**7. Monetization Path**
- Sponsorship: when to start, CPM rates, pitch template
- Premium content: bonus episodes, early access, ad-free
- Merchandise and products
- Live events and meetups
- Course or community tied to podcast
- Affiliate marketing integration

**8. Analytics and Improvement**
- Key metrics: downloads per episode, listener retention, growth rate
- Which episodes performed best and why
- Listener survey (quarterly)
- Audio quality improvement checklist
- Content refinement based on data

Provide specific tool names, pricing, and setup instructions for each budget tier.

Pro Tips for Video & Multimedia

  • Include your equipment setup (phone vs camera, mic type, lighting) so production recommendations match your capabilities.
  • Specify your platform (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels) since optimal format, length, and style differ dramatically.
  • Include your current audience size and engagement rate so the AI tailors growth strategy to your stage.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You want to grow on TikTok or Instagram Reels but do not know what content formats work best for your niche.
  • You are launching a podcast and need everything from concept to launch strategy to growth tactics.
  • You have long-form content (blogs, podcasts, videos) and want to repurpose it into 15+ pieces for other platforms.

Expected Results

  • Complete video scripts with timestamps, B-roll directions, on-screen text cues, and retention hooks.
  • Content calendars with 30 days of specific ideas, hooks, captions, and posting schedules.
  • Launch strategies with pre-launch timelines, day-one tactics, and growth playbooks.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Include your current subscriber or follower count so growth tactics match your stage.
  • Modify the production notes based on your actual equipment and editing capabilities.
  • Add your monetization model (ads, sponsorships, products, courses) so CTAs align with your revenue strategy.
