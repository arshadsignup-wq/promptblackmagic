Product Photography Prompt Generator

Describe your product and get a detailed AI image generation prompt for stunning product shots ready for Midjourney, DALL-E, or Stable Diffusion.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Copy and paste this prompt into ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini. Then paste the output into your AI image generator of choice.

The Prompt

You are an expert commercial photographer and AI image prompt engineer who has directed product shoots for Fortune 500 brands.

My product: [PRODUCT NAME AND DESCRIPTION]
Brand style: [MINIMAL/LUXURY/PLAYFUL/BOLD/ORGANIC]
Platform: [INSTAGRAM/WEBSITE HERO/AMAZON LISTING/PRINT AD]

Generate 5 highly detailed AI image generation prompts for this product. Each prompt must include:

1. **Camera & Lens Spec** - specific camera model, focal length, aperture (e.g., Sony A7IV, 85mm f/1.4)
2. **Lighting Setup** - key light, fill light, rim light, color temperature, modifiers
3. **Background & Surface** - material, color, texture, props
4. **Composition** - rule of thirds placement, angle (flat lay, 45-degree, eye-level, hero angle)
5. **Post-Processing Style** - color grading, contrast, shadows, highlights
6. **Mood Keywords** - 5-8 descriptive words that define the aesthetic

Variations to cover:
- Clean white/studio shot (for e-commerce)
- Lifestyle/in-context shot (product in use)
- Dramatic/moody hero shot (for ads)
- Flat lay with props (for social media)
- Close-up detail/texture shot (for premium feel)

Format each prompt as a single paragraph ready to paste into Midjourney or DALL-E. Add negative prompt suggestions for each.

Pro Tips for Image Generation

  • Always specify the exact camera, lens, and aperture in your prompt - AI image generators produce dramatically better results with technical photography terms.
  • Include negative prompts to exclude unwanted elements like blurry backgrounds, extra fingers, or distorted text in your generated images.
  • Reference specific art styles, lighting setups, and color palettes rather than vague descriptors like "beautiful" or "professional" for more consistent results.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You are designing marketing materials and need eye-catching visuals that match a specific brand aesthetic.
  • You need to visualize a concept, mockup, or prototype before investing in professional production.
  • You are exploring different visual styles for a rebrand and want to rapidly test multiple creative directions.

Expected Results

  • Multiple variations of the same concept covering different styles, angles, and moods for A/B testing.
  • Complete negative prompt suggestions to avoid common AI image artifacts and unwanted elements.
  • Platform-specific aspect ratios and composition guidelines for each intended use case.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Modify the camera and lens specs to match the look you want - wider for environmental, tighter for product detail.
  • Replace the product description with your actual product name, dimensions, materials, and key visual features.
  • Swap the style preference for your brand aesthetic - minimal, luxury, playful, bold, or organic.
