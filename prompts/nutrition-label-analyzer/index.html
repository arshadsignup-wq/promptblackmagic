Nutrition Label Analyzer

Decode food nutrition labels to make informed dietary choices, understand hidden ingredients, and compare products.

By Arshad Hossain

How to Use

Copy & paste the prompt below into your preferred LLM. Unless a specific AI model is mentioned, you can use whichever you prefer.

The Prompt

You are a registered dietitian and food science expert. Analyze this nutrition label.

DISCLAIMER: Educational guidance only. Consult a dietitian for personalized nutrition advice.

Product: [PRODUCT NAME]
Serving Size: [AS LISTED]
Calories: [AMOUNT]
Total Fat: [G] (Saturated: [G], Trans: [G])
Sodium: [MG]
Total Carbs: [G] (Fiber: [G], Total Sugars: [G], Added Sugars: [G])
Protein: [G]
Ingredients: [PASTE FULL LIST]

My dietary goal: [WEIGHT LOSS / MUSCLE GAIN / HEART HEALTH / BLOOD SUGAR / GENERAL HEALTH]
Restrictions: [NONE / VEGETARIAN / VEGAN / GLUTEN-FREE / KETO / DIABETIC / OTHER]

Analyze:
1. **Quick Verdict:** Good choice / neutral / poor choice for your goal. Single most important thing to know
2. **Macro Breakdown:** Calories in context, fat quality assessment, sugar analysis (teaspoons equivalent, added vs. natural), fiber-to-sugar ratio, protein quality, glycemic impact estimate
3. **Sodium Check:** % of daily limit, context for the category
4. **Ingredients Deep Dive:** Explain non-obvious ingredients, flag artificial additives, identify hidden sugars (60+ names), allergen check, ingredient quality rating
5. **Marketing vs. Reality:** Misleading label claims debunked for this product type
6. **Alternatives:** 2-3 healthier alternatives in the same category
7. **Meal Context:** How to incorporate this, what to pair it with, portion adjustments for your goal
8. **Label Literacy Tips:** 5 universal rules for reading any nutrition label

Knowledge is the best diet. Understanding what you eat matters more than counting calories.

Why "Nutrition Label Analyzer" Works

This prompt works across ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini because it uses universal prompting principles - structured enumeration and reasoning elicitation and role assignment - rather than model-specific tricks that break when you switch platforms. You can expect evidence-based plans with progressive overload, recovery scheduling, and modification options - the kind of result that normally requires several rounds of prompt refinement.

These health & fitness tips will help you get stronger results when using "Nutrition Label Analyzer" and similar prompts in this category.

  • Request nutrition guidance alongside workout plans. Exercise without proper nutrition produces minimal results.
  • Ask the AI to create progressive plans that build gradually - jumping into advanced routines causes injury and burnout.

When to Use "Nutrition Label Analyzer"

"Nutrition Label Analyzer" is particularly useful in these situations. If any of these scenarios sound familiar, this prompt will save you significant time.

  • You are training for a specific event like a 5K or marathon and need a progressive training plan with recovery days built in.
  • You want to meal prep for the week and need a shopping list and recipe plan that balances nutrition with time constraints.
  • You are starting a fitness routine after years of inactivity and need a beginner plan that builds consistency without injury.

What You Will Get from "Nutrition Label Analyzer"

When you use "Nutrition Label Analyzer" with ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini, here is what to expect in the AI output.

  • Goal-specific training programs tailored to your target event, body composition, or performance metric.
  • Habit tracking templates that monitor consistency, progression, and recovery across training cycles.
  • Meal prep schedules with grocery lists, recipes, macronutrient breakdowns, and prep time estimates.

How to Customize "Nutrition Label Analyzer"

Adapt "Nutrition Label Analyzer" to your specific situation by modifying these key areas. The more context you add, the better the results.

  • Modify the schedule to reflect how many days per week you can realistically commit to training.
  • Replace the fitness level placeholder with your actual current ability: beginner, intermediate, or advanced.
  • Adjust the equipment references to match what you actually have access to: gym, home dumbbells, or bodyweight only.
