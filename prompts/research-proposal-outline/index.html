Research Proposal Outline

Describe your research idea, methodology, and goals. Get a complete proposal framework with objectives, methods, timeline, and budget.

How to Use

Fill in your research details. Use the outline as a starting point and expand each section with your own data.

The Prompt

Help me outline a research proposal that is thorough and fundable.

Research title (working): [YOUR TITLE]
Field: [YOUR DISCIPLINE]
Type of research: [QUANTITATIVE / QUALITATIVE / MIXED METHODS]
Institution: [UNIVERSITY OR ORGANIZATION]
Funding target: [GRANT NAME OR AMOUNT IF KNOWN]

My research question: [THE CENTRAL QUESTION]
Why it matters: [REAL-WORLD IMPACT OR THEORETICAL SIGNIFICANCE]
What I already know: [BRIEF SUMMARY OF PRELIMINARY FINDINGS OR LITERATURE]

Generate a proposal outline with:

1. Abstract template (300 words): Problem, gap, approach, expected impact

2. Introduction section:
   - Hook: Why should anyone care about this problem?
   - Background: What has been done so far (brief)
   - Gap: What is missing in current knowledge
   - Thesis: What this study will contribute

3. Research objectives: 3-4 SMART objectives (specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, time-bound)

4. Methodology section:
   - Study design and justification
   - Population and sampling strategy
   - Data collection methods and tools
   - Data analysis plan
   - Validity and reliability measures
   - Ethical considerations

5. Timeline: A realistic Gantt chart description broken into phases (literature review, data collection, analysis, writing)

6. Budget outline: Standard line items for my type of research

7. Expected outcomes and significance

8. Potential limitations and how I plan to address them

9. Three things that make a proposal stand out to reviewers in my field
