Brand Logo Concept Generator

Input your brand name and values, then get multiple AI image prompts for logo concepts across different styles - from minimalist to illustrated.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Use with Midjourney or DALL-E 3. Replace bracketed text with your brand details. Generate multiple variations and refine.

The Prompt

You are an award-winning logo designer who has created brand identities for Fortune 500 companies and innovative startups.

Brand name: [YOUR BRAND NAME]
Industry: [WHAT YOUR COMPANY DOES]
Brand values: [3-4 CORE VALUES]
Target audience: [WHO YOUR CUSTOMERS ARE]
Competitor logos to differentiate from: [LIST 2-3 COMPETITORS]
Preferred color direction: [WARM/COOL/MONOCHROME/VIBRANT/EARTH TONES]

Generate 8 distinct logo concept prompts:

**1. Minimalist Wordmark**
- Custom typography, clean lines, no icon
- Focus on letterform uniqueness
- Works at small sizes (favicon, app icon)

**2. Abstract Symbol + Wordmark**
- Geometric or organic abstract mark
- Symbol that works independently from text
- Conveys brand values through shape language

**3. Lettermark / Monogram**
- Initials of the brand name
- Interlocking or overlapping letter design
- Bold and memorable

**4. Illustrated Mascot**
- Character or creature that represents the brand
- Friendly, approachable, distinctive
- Works in multiple contexts (app icon, merch, social)

**5. Negative Space Logo**
- Hidden shape or meaning within the letterforms
- Clever visual double-meaning
- Simple enough to recognize at a glance

**6. Vintage / Heritage Style**
- Badge, crest, or seal format
- Detailed linework, classic typography
- Premium, established feel

**7. Gradient / Modern Tech**
- Bold gradient colors
- 3D or dimensional feel
- Tech-forward, contemporary

**8. Line Art / Continuous Line**
- Single stroke or minimal strokes
- Elegant, artistic feel
- Recognizable silhouette

For each concept:
- Write the complete image generation prompt (40-50 words)
- Specify: style keywords, color codes, background (white/transparent), vector-clean look
- Include negative prompts to avoid: text errors, blurry edges, photorealistic elements
- Add notes on which use cases each concept best serves (web, print, merch, app icon)

Pro Tips for Image Generation

  • Always specify the exact camera, lens, and aperture in your prompt - AI image generators produce dramatically better results with technical photography terms.
  • Include negative prompts to exclude unwanted elements like blurry backgrounds, extra fingers, or distorted text in your generated images.
  • Reference specific art styles, lighting setups, and color palettes rather than vague descriptors like "beautiful" or "professional" for more consistent results.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You need product photography for your e-commerce store but cannot afford a professional photographer or studio rental.
  • You are building a social media presence and need consistent, on-brand visual content across multiple platforms.
  • You want to create unique art or illustrations for a project without hiring a graphic designer.

Expected Results

  • Multiple variations of the same concept covering different styles, angles, and moods for A/B testing.
  • Complete negative prompt suggestions to avoid common AI image artifacts and unwanted elements.
  • Platform-specific aspect ratios and composition guidelines for each intended use case.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Change the platform specification to match where the image will be used (Instagram square, website banner, print ad).
  • Adjust the lighting and mood descriptors to match your brand photography guidelines.
  • Add your brand colors as hex codes so the AI can incorporate them into backgrounds and styling.
