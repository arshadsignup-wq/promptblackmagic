Online-Offline Promo Ads

Combine local awareness, call extensions, and online-to-offline tracking to drive both in-store visits and online sales from one campaign.

By Arshad Hossain

How to Use

Copy & paste the prompt below into your preferred LLM. Unless a specific AI model is mentioned, you can use whichever you prefer.

The Prompt

You are an expert Google Ads marketing specialist. I run a [physical storefront] in addition to an online shop, and I’d like to promote both. Ask me about how far people typically travel to visit my store, any local promotions I’m running, and my current online-to-offline tracking methods. Be sure to discuss whether I have a Google My Business profile and if I use call or location extensions.

Why "Online-Offline Promo Ads" Works

This prompt works across ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini because it uses universal prompting principles - structured enumeration and role assignment - rather than model-specific tricks that break when you switch platforms. Expect keyword-aligned ad components that improve Quality Score and reduce cost per click. The constraints in this prompt prevent the model from falling back on vague, unhelpful responses.

Pro Tips for Using "Online-Offline Promo Ads"

These google ads tips will help you get stronger results when using "Online-Offline Promo Ads" and similar prompts in this category.

  • Request the AI to generate responsive search ad components - multiple headlines and descriptions that Google can mix and match automatically.
  • Always include your target keywords in the prompt so the AI generates ad copy that matches search intent and improves Quality Score.
  • Ask the AI to include a clear call-to-action in every ad variation. CTAs like "Get Started" or "Shop Now" significantly improve click-through rates.

When to Use "Online-Offline Promo Ads"

"Online-Offline Promo Ads" is particularly useful in these situations. If any of these scenarios sound familiar, this prompt will save you significant time.

  • You are expanding into Performance Max campaigns and need asset group copy across multiple formats simultaneously.
  • You are setting up responsive search ads and need 15 headline variations and 4 descriptions that mix well together.
  • You are running campaigns for a local business and need location-specific ad copy that drives foot traffic.

What You Will Get from "Online-Offline Promo Ads"

When you use "Online-Offline Promo Ads" with ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini, here is what to expect in the AI output.

  • Responsive search ad components with 15 headlines and 4 descriptions optimized for keyword relevance.
  • Negative keyword lists organized by theme to eliminate irrelevant clicks and reduce wasted spend.
  • Campaign structure recommendations that group keywords by intent and match type for better performance.

How to Customize "Online-Offline Promo Ads"

Adapt "Online-Offline Promo Ads" to your specific situation by modifying these key areas. The more context you add, the better the results.

  • Include your current Quality Score and CTR benchmarks so the AI targets improvements above your baseline.
  • Replace placeholder keywords with your actual target keywords and their match types for relevant ad copy.
  • Adjust the budget references to match your daily or monthly spend limits for realistic bid strategies.
