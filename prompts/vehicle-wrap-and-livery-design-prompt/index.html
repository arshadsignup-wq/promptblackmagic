Vehicle Wrap and Livery Design Prompt

Describe your vehicle and brand, then get AI prompts for professional vehicle wrap designs - from subtle branding to full graphic wraps.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Use with Midjourney or DALL-E 3 to visualize concepts. Share renderings with your wrap installer for production.

The Prompt

You are a vehicle wrap designer who has created award-winning fleet graphics, racing liveries, and branded vehicle designs for Fortune 500 companies.

Vehicle type: [CAR/VAN/TRUCK/BUS/TRAILER/MOTORCYCLE/BOAT]
Vehicle model: [SPECIFIC MAKE AND MODEL]
Brand/company: [BUSINESS NAME AND INDUSTRY]
Brand colors: [PRIMARY, SECONDARY, ACCENT COLORS]
Design goal: [BRAND AWARENESS/LEAD GENERATION/RACING TEAM/PERSONAL/FOOD TRUCK]

Generate 5 vehicle wrap design prompts:

**1. Full Wrap - Bold Brand Statement**
- Entire vehicle covered in branded graphics
- Logo prominent on sides, rear, and hood/roof
- Contact info (phone, website) clearly readable
- Design flows with the vehicle's body lines
- Door handles, mirrors, and windows accounted for

**2. Partial Wrap - Professional & Clean**
- 50-60% coverage, original paint visible
- Graphics on lower half or specific panels
- More conservative, executive appearance
- Strategic placement for maximum visibility
- Clean transitions between wrap and paint

**3. Racing / Performance Livery**
- Motorsport-inspired graphics
- Sponsor placement areas
- Number panel placement
- Aggressive, speed-suggesting design elements
- Stripe and accent placement following body contours

**4. Minimal / Luxury Approach**
- Subtle color change wrap (matte, satin, or color shift)
- Small logo or wordmark placement
- Premium material simulation (brushed metal, carbon fiber)
- Less is more philosophy
- High-end, exclusive feeling

**5. Rear and Side Focus (Fleet Standard)**
- Designed for traffic visibility (vehicle in front of you)
- Large rear graphic with clear messaging
- Side panels with services and contact info
- Consistent across a fleet of different vehicle sizes
- Template-based for easy reproduction

For each prompt: show the design applied to the specific vehicle model (not flat), include multiple angles (front 3/4, rear 3/4, side profile), specify wrap material finish (gloss, matte, satin), and note areas to avoid wrapping (windows, lights, vents). Include vehicle color under the wrap for partial designs.

Pro Tips for Image Generation

  • Include negative prompts to exclude unwanted elements like blurry backgrounds, extra fingers, or distorted text in your generated images.
  • Reference specific art styles, lighting setups, and color palettes rather than vague descriptors like "beautiful" or "professional" for more consistent results.
  • Specify the aspect ratio and intended use (Instagram post, website hero, print) so the composition works for your platform.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You want to create unique art or illustrations for a project without hiring a graphic designer.
  • You are designing marketing materials and need eye-catching visuals that match a specific brand aesthetic.
  • You need to visualize a concept, mockup, or prototype before investing in professional production.

Expected Results

  • Complete negative prompt suggestions to avoid common AI image artifacts and unwanted elements.
  • Platform-specific aspect ratios and composition guidelines for each intended use case.
  • Color palette recommendations with hex codes that complement the subject and intended brand aesthetic.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Add your brand colors as hex codes so the AI can incorporate them into backgrounds and styling.
  • Modify the camera and lens specs to match the look you want - wider for environmental, tighter for product detail.
  • Replace the product description with your actual product name, dimensions, materials, and key visual features.
