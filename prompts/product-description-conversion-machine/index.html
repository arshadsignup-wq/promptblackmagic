Product Description Conversion Machine

Enter product specs and get conversion-optimized descriptions for your store, Amazon, social media, and ads - all from one prompt.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into any LLM with your product details. Get back descriptions for every platform at once.

The Prompt

You are a direct-response copywriter who has written product descriptions generating over $50M in e-commerce revenue.

Product name: [NAME]
Product type: [CATEGORY]
Price: [PRICE]
Key features: [LIST 5-7 FEATURES]
Target customer: [WHO BUYS THIS AND WHY]
Pain point it solves: [WHAT PROBLEM DOES IT FIX]
Competitor products: [WHAT ELSE THEY MIGHT BUY INSTEAD]

Write conversion-optimized copy for every platform:

**1. Shopify/Website Product Page**
- Headline (benefit-driven, not feature-driven)
- Subheadline (specificity and credibility)
- 5 bullet points (feature > benefit > proof format)
- Long description (300 words, storytelling approach)
- Technical specifications table
- Social proof integration points

**2. Amazon Listing**
- Title (keyword-optimized, under 200 characters)
- 5 bullet points (Amazon format: CAPS BENEFIT - supporting detail)
- A+ Content description sections (3 modules)
- Backend search terms (250 characters)

**3. Social Media Product Posts**
- Instagram caption (with hooks, hashtags, CTA)
- TikTok script (15-second product showcase)
- Facebook marketplace description
- Pinterest product pin description

**4. Ad Copy Variations**
- Facebook/Instagram ad (primary text + headline + description, 3 variations)
- Google Shopping title and description
- Google Search ad (3 headlines + 2 descriptions)

**5. Email Copy**
- Product launch email (subject + body)
- Product feature spotlight email
- 'Last chance' urgency email

For ALL copy: use sensory language, specific numbers over vague claims, address objections within the copy, and include clear CTAs. No fluff words ('amazing', 'incredible', 'revolutionary').

Pro Tips for E-Commerce

  • Include your shipping model and return policy details since they significantly impact conversion and copy strategy.
  • Request mobile-specific optimizations separately - mobile e-commerce has different UX requirements than desktop.
  • Include your average order value and current conversion rate so the AI can prioritize high-impact recommendations.

When to Use This Prompt

  • Your product descriptions are not converting and you need persuasive copy optimized for each sales channel.
  • Your cart abandonment rate is above 70% and you need a multi-channel recovery system to recapture lost sales.
  • You are expanding to new sales channels (Amazon, social commerce) and need platform-specific product copy.

Expected Results

  • A/B test recommendations with specific variables to test and success metrics to track.
  • Platform-specific product copy optimized for Shopify, Amazon, and social commerce channels.
  • Multi-channel cart recovery sequences with email copy, SMS messages, and retargeting ad text.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Include your shipping model, return policy, and payment options for accurate checkout optimization.
  • Modify the pricing and discount strategy to match your margins and competitive positioning.
  • Add your current traffic and conversion data so recommendations prioritize the highest-impact changes.
