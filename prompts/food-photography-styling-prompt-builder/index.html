Food Photography Styling Prompt Builder

Name a dish and get detailed AI prompts for professional food photography with specific styling, props, lighting, and composition directions.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into Midjourney or DALL-E 3. Customize the dish and styling preferences in brackets.

The Prompt

You are a food photographer and stylist who shoots for Bon Appetit, Food & Wine, and premium restaurant brands.

Dish: [SPECIFIC DISH NAME]
Cuisine type: [ITALIAN/JAPANESE/MEXICAN/FRENCH/AMERICAN/OTHER]
Mood: [RUSTIC/ELEGANT/BRIGHT & FRESH/MOODY & DARK/PLAYFUL]
Shoot purpose: [RESTAURANT MENU/COOKBOOK/SOCIAL MEDIA/AD CAMPAIGN/FOOD BLOG]

Create 5 food photography prompts:

**1. Hero Flat Lay (Top-Down)**
- 90-degree overhead angle
- Styled with complementary ingredients scattered around
- Include specific props: linen napkin texture, wooden board, scattered herbs, olive oil drizzle
- Natural light from one direction creating soft shadows
- Specify plate style, surface material (marble, dark wood, concrete)

**2. 45-Degree Hero Shot**
- Classic food magazine angle
- Steam or motion (cheese pull, sauce pour, crumb break)
- Shallow depth of field with background plates soft
- Garnish placement and sauce swoosh details
- Warm, appetizing color temperature

**3. Close-Up Texture Shot**
- Macro view showing texture - crispy edges, melted cheese, glaze, crumb structure
- Extreme shallow depth of field
- Backlight to create translucency in thin ingredients
- Highlight the most crave-worthy element

**4. Process / Action Shot**
- Hands in frame (sprinkling salt, slicing, plating)
- Motion blur on the action, sharp on the food
- Kitchen or cooking environment background
- Documentary, authentic feel

**5. Dark & Moody Still Life**
- Inspired by Dutch Golden Age paintings
- Rich, dark background (charcoal, deep navy)
- Dramatic side lighting with deep shadows
- Vintage props: aged utensils, ceramic bowls, raw ingredients
- Painterly quality

For each prompt: write as a single paragraph optimized for AI image generation. Include specific color palette, lighting direction, lens equivalent, and styling details that make the food look irresistible.

Pro Tips for Image Generation

  • Include negative prompts to exclude unwanted elements like blurry backgrounds, extra fingers, or distorted text in your generated images.
  • Reference specific art styles, lighting setups, and color palettes rather than vague descriptors like "beautiful" or "professional" for more consistent results.
  • Specify the aspect ratio and intended use (Instagram post, website hero, print) so the composition works for your platform.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You are building a social media presence and need consistent, on-brand visual content across multiple platforms.
  • You want to create unique art or illustrations for a project without hiring a graphic designer.
  • You are designing marketing materials and need eye-catching visuals that match a specific brand aesthetic.

Expected Results

  • Complete negative prompt suggestions to avoid common AI image artifacts and unwanted elements.
  • Platform-specific aspect ratios and composition guidelines for each intended use case.
  • Color palette recommendations with hex codes that complement the subject and intended brand aesthetic.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Adjust the lighting and mood descriptors to match your brand photography guidelines.
  • Add your brand colors as hex codes so the AI can incorporate them into backgrounds and styling.
  • Modify the camera and lens specs to match the look you want - wider for environmental, tighter for product detail.
