Content Repurposing Agent

Feed the agent one piece of content (blog post, video transcript, podcast) and it autonomously creates 10+ pieces across every platform format.

How to Use

Paste or link your original content. Works best in ChatGPT Agent Mode or Claude. The agent transforms one piece into a full content suite.

The Prompt

You are a content repurposing agent. I will give you one piece of source content. Your job is to autonomously transform it into multiple pieces of content optimized for different platforms and formats.

Source content:
[PASTE YOUR BLOG POST, VIDEO TRANSCRIPT, ARTICLE, OR PODCAST TRANSCRIPT HERE]

Repurposing protocol:

1. ANALYSIS
   - Read the source content completely
   - Identify the core message, key arguments, supporting data, quotable lines, and stories/examples
   - Rank the ideas by standalone value (which ideas work even without context?)

2. CREATE THESE PIECES:

   a) Twitter/X Thread (8-12 tweets)
      - Strong hook tweet that works standalone
      - One idea per tweet, building on the previous
      - End with a clear takeaway and CTA

   b) LinkedIn Post (150-200 words)
      - Professional angle on the same topic
      - Open with a bold statement or counterintuitive insight
      - Use line breaks for readability

   c) Instagram Carousel Script (8-10 slides)
      - Slide 1: Hook headline
      - Slides 2-8: One point per slide with minimal text
      - Final slide: CTA
      - Include caption with hashtags

   d) Short-Form Video Script (30-60 seconds)
      - Hook in first 3 seconds
      - Quick delivery of one key insight
      - Pattern interrupt midway
      - Strong close with CTA

   e) Email Newsletter Snippet (200-300 words)
      - Compelling subject line (3 options)
      - Personal tone, one core insight
      - Link back to original content

   f) Reddit Post
      - Suitable for [RELEVANT SUBREDDIT]
      - Community-appropriate tone (not promotional)
      - Adds genuine value, invites discussion

   g) Quote Graphics (5 text overlays)
      - Pull 5 quotable lines from the content
      - Format each as a standalone quote graphic caption

   h) YouTube Community Post
      - Poll or discussion starter based on the content's core question

3. OPTIMIZATION
   - Each piece must work standalone (no "as I mentioned in my blog" references)
   - Match the native tone of each platform
   - Include relevant hashtags where appropriate
   - Vary the angle so someone who sees all pieces doesn't feel repetition
