Real Estate Market Analysis Report

Name a market and get a framework for analyzing it - supply and demand indicators, price trends, demographic shifts, and investment opportunity assessment.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into any LLM with the market details. Use the output to evaluate markets for investment, relocation, or business expansion.

The Prompt

You are a real estate market analyst who advises institutional investors, developers, and REITs on market selection and timing decisions.

Market: [CITY OR METRO AREA]
Analysis purpose: [INVESTMENT/RELOCATION/DEVELOPMENT/MARKET ENTRY/CLIENT ADVISORY]
Property focus: [RESIDENTIAL/COMMERCIAL/INDUSTRIAL/MULTI-FAMILY/ALL]
Time horizon: [1 YEAR/3 YEARS/5+ YEARS]

Create a comprehensive market analysis framework:

**1. Demographic Analysis**
- Population growth rate and trends (5-year, 10-year)
- Age distribution and household formation
- Income levels and growth
- Employment sectors and major employers
- Net migration patterns (where are people coming from/going)
- Education levels
- Remote work impact on population

**2. Economic Indicators**
- Job growth by sector
- Unemployment rate vs national average
- Major employer stability and expansion plans
- GDP growth and economic diversification
- Cost of living index
- Business-friendly policies and tax environment
- Infrastructure projects (transit, highway, airport)

**3. Supply Analysis**
- Current housing inventory (months of supply)
- New construction permits and pipeline
- Building restrictions and zoning
- Land availability and cost
- Construction cost trends
- Conversion activity (office to residential, etc.)

**4. Demand Indicators**
- Days on market (DOM) trend
- List-to-sale price ratio
- Absorption rate
- Buyer competition (multiple offers, bidding wars)
- Mortgage application volume
- Rental occupancy rates
- Waiting lists for rentals

**5. Price Analysis**
- Median home price and year-over-year change
- Price per square foot by neighborhood
- Price tier analysis (starter, mid-range, luxury)
- Rent growth rate
- Rent-to-price ratio (rental yield indicator)
- Affordability index
- Price comparison to comparable metros

**6. Neighborhood Ranking**
- Top 5 neighborhoods for investment (and why)
- Top 5 neighborhoods for appreciation (and why)
- Up-and-coming areas to watch (early indicators)
- Areas to avoid (and why)
- Scoring criteria: price trend, crime, schools, walkability, transit

**7. Risk Factors**
- Natural disaster risk (flood, fire, earthquake, hurricane)
- Regulatory risk (rent control, zoning changes, tenant protections)
- Economic concentration risk (one-employer town)
- Oversupply risk (too much construction)
- Interest rate sensitivity
- Political and policy environment

**8. Market Cycle Position**
- Where is this market in the cycle? (recovery, expansion, hyper-supply, recession)
- Leading indicators to watch
- Timing recommendation
- Comparison to previous cycles

Provide specific data sources for each metric. Include a market scorecard with weighted criteria and an overall investment rating.

Pro Tips for Real Estate

  • Specify your local market conditions (buyer/seller market, inventory levels) for more relevant pricing and strategy advice.
  • Ask the AI to include specific financial calculations with formulas shown so you can verify the math independently.
  • Include comparable property data when available so the AI can provide market-aware recommendations.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You are analyzing whether to buy or rent in your market and need an objective financial comparison.
  • You are preparing a comparative market analysis and need a structured approach to pricing a property accurately.
  • You are listing a property and need compelling description copy that drives showings and offers.

Expected Results

  • Multi-channel marketing copy (social media, email, print) tailored to the property and buyer profile.
  • Step-by-step operational playbooks with scripts, templates, and follow-up sequences.
  • Legal-aware documentation templates with jurisdiction-specific compliance reminders.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Replace the property details with your actual address, specs, features, and recent upgrades.
  • Swap the financial figures with your real purchase price, rates, taxes, and rental estimates.
  • Adjust the target buyer or investor profile to match your specific market and property positioning.
