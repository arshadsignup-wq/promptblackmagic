Client Onboarding Email Sequence

Describe your service and project type. Get a complete 4-email onboarding sequence from welcome to kickoff, with questionnaire questions included.

How to Use

Customize with your business details. Save as reusable templates in your email client for every new client.

The Prompt

Create a complete client onboarding email sequence for my freelance business.

My details:
- My profession: [e.g., graphic designer / web developer / copywriter / consultant]
- Project type: [e.g., brand identity package / website redesign / content strategy]
- Typical project duration: [e.g., 2-4 weeks]
- My communication preferences: [e.g., email + Slack, response within 24 hours, available Mon-Fri 9-5]

Write 4 emails:

Email 1: Welcome and Next Steps
- Thank them for choosing me
- Outline what happens next in the process
- Set expectations for communication (preferred channels, response times, availability hours)
- Mention the onboarding questionnaire they will receive next

Email 2: Project Kickoff Details
- Confirm the project scope, deliverables, and timeline with specific milestones
- Confirm payment schedule
- Request any assets, brand guidelines, login credentials, or materials I need
- Set the date for our kickoff call

Email 3: Onboarding Questionnaire
- Generate 12-15 targeted questions specific to my profession and project type
- Questions should cover: their brand, target audience, preferences, competitors, goals, inspiration, and constraints
- Format as a numbered list they can reply to directly

Email 4: First Progress Update Template
- What I have completed so far
- What is coming next
- Any decisions or input needed from them
- Next milestone date

Keep each email under 200 words. Include subject lines. Tone: professional but warm, organized but not robotic.
