Creative Story Starter

Choose a genre, mood, and setting. Get a compelling story opening with characters, conflict, and a hook that makes readers need to keep going.

How to Use

Pick your genre and style preferences. Use the generated opening as a launching point for your story.

The Prompt

Write a compelling story opening that hooks the reader and sets up an irresistible narrative.

Genre: [THRILLER / SCI-FI / ROMANCE / FANTASY / HORROR / LITERARY FICTION / MYSTERY]
Mood: [DARK / HOPEFUL / TENSE / WHIMSICAL / MELANCHOLIC / ADVENTUROUS]
Setting: [TIME PERIOD AND LOCATION]
Perspective: [FIRST PERSON / THIRD PERSON / UNRELIABLE NARRATOR]
Target audience: [YOUNG ADULT / ADULT / MIDDLE GRADE]

Provide:

1. The opening scene (500-700 words):
   - Start in medias res (in the middle of action or a moment of change)
   - Introduce the protagonist through their actions, not description
   - Plant the central conflict within the first 3 paragraphs
   - End with a micro-cliffhanger that makes the reader turn the page
   - Show the character's voice and personality through dialogue or internal thought

2. Character profile for the protagonist:
   - Name, age, and one defining physical detail
   - Their want (what they are pursuing) vs. their need (what they actually need)
   - Their fatal flaw
   - The lie they believe about themselves

3. Story seed:
   - The central conflict in one sentence
   - Three possible plot directions this opening could lead to
   - The thematic question the story explores (e.g., "Can love survive betrayal?")

4. Craft notes:
   - Why the opening works (which techniques were used)
   - Common mistakes in this genre to avoid
   - Suggested pacing for the next three scenes
