Paste any long-form content. Get it repurposed into 10+ formats: social posts, email, video script, infographic outline, and thread.

How to Use

Paste your blog post, podcast transcript, or article. Get multiple content pieces ready for different platforms.

The Prompt

Take this single piece of content and repurpose it into multiple formats for different platforms.

Original content:
[PASTE YOUR BLOG POST, ARTICLE, PODCAST TRANSCRIPT, OR VIDEO SCRIPT]

Repurpose into:

1. Twitter/X thread (8-12 tweets):
   - First tweet must hook with a bold claim or surprising stat
   - Each tweet should standalone but also flow as a narrative
   - Last tweet should have a CTA

2. LinkedIn post (long-form):
   - Hook first line (triggers "see more" click)
   - Story format with line breaks
   - End with a question to drive comments

3. Instagram carousel (8-10 slides):
   - Slide 1: Hook headline
   - Slides 2-8: Key points with one idea per slide
   - Slide 9: Summary
   - Slide 10: CTA (save, share, follow)
   - Caption with relevant hashtags

4. Email newsletter version (under 300 words):
   - Subject line
   - Personal anecdote opening
   - 3 key takeaways
   - Link to full content

5. YouTube Shorts / TikTok script (60 seconds):
   - Hook (first 3 seconds)
   - Core message
   - Surprising element or pattern interrupt
   - CTA

6. Infographic outline:
   - Title
   - 5-7 data points or key facts to visualize
   - Suggested layout flow

7. Podcast episode talking points:
   - 3-5 discussion questions this content raises
   - Personal stories to add
   - Call-in question for the audience
