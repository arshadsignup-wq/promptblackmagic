Checkout Flow Friction Eliminator

Describe your checkout process and get a step-by-step redesign that reduces cart abandonment and increases completion rates.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into any LLM with your current checkout details. Apply the recommendations to reduce abandonment.

The Prompt

You are a checkout optimization specialist. You've reduced cart abandonment rates by 30-50% for dozens of e-commerce brands.

Store platform: [SHOPIFY/WOOCOMMERCE/CUSTOM]
Current checkout steps: [DESCRIBE YOUR CURRENT FLOW]
Cart abandonment rate: [IF KNOWN OR 'UNKNOWN']
Average order value: [AOV]
Top customer complaint: [IF KNOWN]

Redesign my checkout for maximum conversion:

**1. Cart Page Optimization**
- Cart layout (what to show, what to hide)
- Order summary clarity
- Discount code field (where and how to display without hurting margins)
- Cart abandonment triggers to remove
- Cross-sell placement (without distracting from checkout)

**2. Checkout Flow Architecture**
- Single page vs multi-step (recommendation with reasoning)
- Field reduction audit (which fields to remove, combine, or auto-fill)
- Guest checkout vs account creation strategy
- Progress indicator design
- Form field order optimization

**3. Payment Section**
- Payment method display order (based on conversion data)
- Express checkout buttons (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Shop Pay) placement
- Buy Now Pay Later integration strategy
- Security badge placement near payment fields
- Credit card form UX (auto-format, real-time validation)

**4. Shipping Section**
- Address auto-complete implementation
- Shipping options display (free shipping threshold, delivery dates)
- In-store pickup option
- Tax calculation transparency

**5. Trust Elements**
- Security messaging (exactly what to say and where)
- Money-back guarantee placement
- SSL and payment processor logos
- Customer service access during checkout

**6. Abandonment Recovery**
- Exit-intent strategy
- Abandoned cart email sequence (3 emails with subject lines and copy)
- SMS recovery message
- Retargeting ad copy for cart abandoners

**7. Post-Purchase**
- Order confirmation page optimization (upsell, referral, social share)
- Confirmation email template
- Delivery tracking experience

Provide specific copy for all CTAs, error messages, and trust elements.

Pro Tips for UI/UX Design

  • Request specific component specifications (spacing, typography scale, color tokens) rather than vague layout descriptions.
  • Include your current conversion rate or usability issue so the AI can target specific improvements rather than generic best practices.
  • Include your target user persona and their primary task when prompting for design recommendations - context drives better UX advice.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You are building a SaaS dashboard and need to organize complex data into an intuitive, actionable interface.
  • Your checkout flow has a high abandonment rate and you need specific fixes to reduce friction.
  • You are launching a new product and need a landing page that converts visitors into customers or leads.

Expected Results

  • Design system tokens including typography scales, spacing systems, and color semantics.
  • Detailed page layouts with section-by-section wireframe descriptions, component specs, and content hierarchy.
  • Conversion-optimized copy for headlines, CTAs, error messages, and micro-copy throughout the interface.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Replace the product description with your actual product, its core value proposition, and primary user action.
  • Swap the target audience with your specific user persona including their technical sophistication level.
  • Change the platform specification to match your tech stack (React, Shopify, WordPress, custom).
