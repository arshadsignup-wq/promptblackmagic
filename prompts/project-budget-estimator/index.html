Project Budget Estimator

Enter your project type, team size, and duration. Get a detailed budget with labor, tech, contingency, and monthly cash flow projections.

How to Use

Adjust the line items and rates to match your organization. Present the budget to your sponsor with contingency analysis included.

The Prompt

Create a detailed project budget estimate for a [PROJECT TYPE, e.g., customer-facing web application rebuild].

Parameters:
- Project duration: [e.g., 6 months]
- Team size: [e.g., 8 people across development, design, QA, and PM]
- Key technologies/tools: [e.g., React, Node.js, AWS, Figma]
- Location: [e.g., US-based team / mixed global / offshore]

Include these cost categories with line-item detail:

1. Labor Costs: Broken down by role, hourly/monthly rate, and duration of involvement

2. Technology and Infrastructure: Software licenses, cloud hosting, development tools, CI/CD pipeline costs

3. Third-Party Services: APIs, integrations, external consultants

4. Quality Assurance: Testing tools, device lab, accessibility audits

5. Training and Change Management: Documentation, user training sessions

6. Contingency Reserve: Calculate at 10-15% of total with justification for the percentage chosen

7. Operational/Overhead: Communication tools, travel if applicable

For each category, provide:
- Estimated cost (low / expected / high range)
- Assumptions behind the estimate
- Key cost drivers and risks

Include:
- A budget summary table
- Monthly cash flow projection
- 3 cost optimization strategies that could reduce the total by 10-20% without compromising quality
