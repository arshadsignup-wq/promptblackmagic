Anime Character Design Sheet Generator

Describe a character concept and get detailed prompts for a full anime character design sheet with multiple views, expressions, and outfit variations.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Use with Midjourney, Stable Diffusion (anime models), or NovelAI. Fill in the character details in brackets.

The Prompt

You are a professional anime character designer who has worked on major anime series and gacha game character designs.

Character concept: [NAME AND ROLE - e.g., "fire mage princess" or "cyberpunk bounty hunter"]
Age/build: [AGE RANGE AND BODY TYPE]
Personality traits: [3-4 KEY PERSONALITY TRAITS]
Setting/world: [FANTASY/SCI-FI/MODERN/HISTORICAL]
Art style reference: [STUDIO GHIBLI/SHONEN/SEINEN/CHIBI/OTHER]

Create a complete character design prompt set:

**1. Full Body Turnaround (Front/Side/Back)**
- T-pose or relaxed standing pose
- Clean lines, flat color, white background
- Detailed costume design with material annotations
- Hair physics and flow direction
- Include height reference and proportions (head-to-body ratio)

**2. Expression Sheet (6 Emotions)**
- Happy, angry, sad, surprised, determined, embarrassed
- Consistent face shape and features across all expressions
- Close-up bust shots, 2x3 grid layout
- Show how hair and accessories move with expressions

**3. Signature Pose / Action Shot**
- Dynamic pose showing character in their element
- Special effects (magic, weapons, aura) if applicable
- Dramatic angle and lighting
- Background that reflects their world

**4. Outfit Variations (3 designs)**
- Default / everyday outfit
- Battle / work outfit
- Casual / off-duty outfit
- Color palette swatch for each

**5. Weapon/Item Design**
- Character's signature weapon or tool
- Multiple angles and detail views
- Material callouts and design motifs

**6. Chibi/SD Version**
- 2-3 head tall super-deformed version
- 3 cute poses or reactions
- Simplified but recognizable design elements

Write each as a standalone prompt. Include art style keywords, quality tags, and negative prompts for each.

Pro Tips for Image Generation

  • Describe the mood with 5-8 specific adjectives rather than generic terms - "warm golden hour glow with soft bokeh" beats "nice lighting" every time.
  • Always specify the exact camera, lens, and aperture in your prompt - AI image generators produce dramatically better results with technical photography terms.
  • Include negative prompts to exclude unwanted elements like blurry backgrounds, extra fingers, or distorted text in your generated images.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You are exploring different visual styles for a rebrand and want to rapidly test multiple creative directions.
  • You need product photography for your e-commerce store but cannot afford a professional photographer or studio rental.
  • You are building a social media presence and need consistent, on-brand visual content across multiple platforms.

Expected Results

  • Detailed, copy-paste ready prompts for AI image generators with camera settings, lighting specs, and style keywords.
  • Multiple variations of the same concept covering different styles, angles, and moods for A/B testing.
  • Complete negative prompt suggestions to avoid common AI image artifacts and unwanted elements.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Swap the style preference for your brand aesthetic - minimal, luxury, playful, bold, or organic.
  • Change the platform specification to match where the image will be used (Instagram square, website banner, print ad).
  • Adjust the lighting and mood descriptors to match your brand photography guidelines.
