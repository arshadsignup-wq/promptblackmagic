E-Commerce Store Launch Checklist

Describe your product and get a complete store launch plan - from product page setup to payment processing to day-one marketing strategy.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into any LLM with your store details. Use the output as a step-by-step launch guide.

The Prompt

You are an e-commerce consultant who has launched 200+ online stores, from Shopify dropshipping operations to $50M DTC brands.

Product: [WHAT YOU'RE SELLING]
Platform: [SHOPIFY/WOOCOMMERCE/BIGCOMMERCE/SQUARESPACE/CUSTOM]
Business model: [DTC/DROPSHIP/PRINT-ON-DEMAND/SUBSCRIPTION/MARKETPLACE]
Budget: [BOOTSTRAP/SMALL/SIGNIFICANT]
Launch timeline: [2 WEEKS/1 MONTH/3 MONTHS]

Create a complete store launch plan:

**1. Store Setup Essentials**
- Domain and hosting configuration
- Theme selection criteria (speed, mobile, customization)
- Essential pages: homepage, product, collection, about, contact, FAQ, policies
- Legal pages: privacy policy, terms of service, return policy, shipping policy
- Navigation structure (header, footer, mobile)
- Currency, tax, and shipping zone configuration

**2. Product Pages (Conversion-Optimized)**
- Product title format (brand + product + key feature)
- Description structure (benefit headline, features, specs, social proof)
- Image requirements (main, lifestyle, detail, size reference - 6-8 per product)
- Variant selection UX (color swatches, size guide integration)
- Price display (compare-at, installment options)
- Trust badges placement
- Reviews section setup
- Cross-sell and upsell strategy

**3. Checkout Optimization**
- Guest checkout enabled
- Express payment (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Shop Pay)
- Abandoned cart email sequence (3 emails, timing, content)
- Post-purchase upsell
- Order confirmation email customization
- Shipping notification setup

**4. Payment and Fulfillment**
- Payment processor selection and setup
- Shipping carrier integration
- Shipping rate strategy (free threshold, flat rate, calculated)
- Fulfillment workflow (pick, pack, ship process)
- Returns process and prepaid label setup
- Inventory management system

**5. Analytics and Tracking**
- Google Analytics 4 setup with e-commerce events
- Meta Pixel with conversion API
- Google Ads conversion tracking
- Heatmap tool installation
- UTM parameter strategy
- KPI dashboard setup (revenue, AOV, conversion rate, CAC)

**6. Pre-Launch Marketing**
- Coming soon page with email capture
- Social media profile setup and content calendar
- Influencer seeding strategy
- Launch day promotion plan
- Email list warming (if existing list)
- PR outreach for launch coverage

**7. Launch Day Checklist**
- [ ] All links work
- [ ] Test purchase completed on every payment method
- [ ] Mobile experience verified
- [ ] Page speed acceptable (under 3 seconds)
- [ ] Tracking firing correctly
- [ ] Email automations tested
- [ ] Customer support channel live
- [ ] Social media announcement queued
- [ ] Paid ads activated
- [ ] Team notified and ready

**8. Post-Launch Week 1 Priorities**
- Monitor for checkout issues
- Respond to customer inquiries quickly
- Gather first reviews
- Analyze traffic sources
- Optimize based on real data

Provide specific tool recommendations, plugin names, and configuration settings for each step.

Pro Tips for E-Commerce

  • Include your shipping model and return policy details since they significantly impact conversion and copy strategy.
  • Request mobile-specific optimizations separately - mobile e-commerce has different UX requirements than desktop.
  • Include your average order value and current conversion rate so the AI can prioritize high-impact recommendations.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You want to increase average order value and need cross-sell and upsell strategies for your product pages.
  • Your checkout flow has too many steps and you need to streamline it for higher completion rates.
  • You are launching a Shopify store and need a comprehensive checklist to avoid costly mistakes at launch.

Expected Results

  • A/B test recommendations with specific variables to test and success metrics to track.
  • Platform-specific product copy optimized for Shopify, Amazon, and social commerce channels.
  • Multi-channel cart recovery sequences with email copy, SMS messages, and retargeting ad text.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Replace the product details with your actual product name, specs, price, and unique selling points.
  • Swap the platform references to match your actual store setup (Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce).
  • Adjust the target customer profile to match your actual buyer demographics and purchase behavior.
