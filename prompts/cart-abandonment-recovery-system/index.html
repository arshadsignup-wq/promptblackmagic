Cart Abandonment Recovery System

Get a complete cart abandonment recovery system with email sequences, SMS messages, retargeting ad copy, and exit-intent strategies.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into any LLM with your store details. Implement across email, SMS, and ads to recover lost sales.

The Prompt

You are an e-commerce retention specialist who recovers 15-25% of abandoned carts (vs the 5-10% industry average).

Store: [STORE NAME AND URL]
Average order value: [AOV]
Average cart abandonment rate: [IF KNOWN]
Products: [WHAT YOU SELL]
Current recovery efforts: [WHAT YOU'RE DOING NOW, OR 'NOTHING']

Build a complete cart abandonment recovery system:

**1. Exit-Intent Strategy**
- Pop-up copy (3 variations to test)
- Discount vs no-discount approach (recommendation based on AOV)
- Design recommendations
- Mobile vs desktop treatment

**2. Email Recovery Sequence (4 emails)**
- Email 1 (1 hour): Reminder - subject line, preview, body, CTA
- Email 2 (24 hours): Social proof - subject line, preview, body, CTA
- Email 3 (48 hours): Objection handling - subject line, preview, body, CTA
- Email 4 (72 hours): Final incentive - subject line, preview, body, CTA
- For each: exact copy, design layout description, dynamic product block placement

**3. SMS Recovery (2 messages)**
- SMS 1 (2 hours): Short, personal, with cart link
- SMS 2 (24 hours): Urgency or incentive
- Character count, personalization tokens, opt-out compliance

**4. Retargeting Ads**
- Facebook/Instagram dynamic product ad copy (3 variations)
- Google Display remarketing ad copy
- Audience segmentation (by cart value, product type, new vs returning)
- Frequency cap recommendations
- Duration of retargeting window

**5. On-Site Recovery**
- Browser push notification copy
- Persistent cart reminder bar
- Wishlist/save-for-later functionality
- Live chat trigger for high-value carts

**6. Analysis & Optimization**
- Why people abandon (top 10 reasons with solutions)
- A/B tests to run (subject lines, incentive amounts, timing)
- Revenue attribution model
- Monthly recovery rate targets

Include all copy ready to deploy. Specify dynamic merge tags.

Pro Tips for E-Commerce

  • Request mobile-specific optimizations separately - mobile e-commerce has different UX requirements than desktop.
  • Include your average order value and current conversion rate so the AI can prioritize high-impact recommendations.
  • Specify your platform (Shopify, WooCommerce, custom) since optimization tactics differ by platform capabilities.

When to Use This Prompt

  • Your cart abandonment rate is above 70% and you need a multi-channel recovery system to recapture lost sales.
  • You are expanding to new sales channels (Amazon, social commerce) and need platform-specific product copy.
  • You want to increase average order value and need cross-sell and upsell strategies for your product pages.

Expected Results

  • Platform-specific product copy optimized for Shopify, Amazon, and social commerce channels.
  • Multi-channel cart recovery sequences with email copy, SMS messages, and retargeting ad text.
  • Pre-launch checklists with priority rankings (critical, important, nice-to-have) for each item.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Modify the pricing and discount strategy to match your margins and competitive positioning.
  • Add your current traffic and conversion data so recommendations prioritize the highest-impact changes.
  • Replace the product details with your actual product name, specs, price, and unique selling points.
