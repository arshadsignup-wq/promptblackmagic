Podcast Launch & Growth Blueprint

Get a complete podcast strategy from concept to launch to growth, including episode structures, guest outreach, and monetization.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into any LLM with your podcast concept. Follow the blueprint from pre-launch through growth phase.

The Prompt

You are a podcast strategist who has launched shows that reached top 10 in their category within 90 days.

Podcast concept: [WHAT YOUR SHOW IS ABOUT]
Target listener: [WHO IS YOUR IDEAL LISTENER]
Format preference: [SOLO/INTERVIEW/CO-HOST/HYBRID]
Episode frequency: [WEEKLY/BIWEEKLY]
Goal: [BUILD AUTHORITY/GENERATE LEADS/MONETIZE/NETWORK]
Budget: [MINIMAL/MODERATE/PROFESSIONAL]

Build a complete podcast blueprint:

**1. Show Foundation**
- Show name options (5 names with reasoning)
- Podcast description (SEO-optimized for Apple/Spotify)
- Category and subcategory selection
- Cover art direction (what works in podcast thumbnails)
- Intro/outro script (15-20 seconds each)
- Music and sound design direction

**2. Episode Structure Template**
- Cold open / teaser (30 seconds)
- Intro and episode context (1 minute)
- Main content structure (segments, timing)
- Mid-roll break points
- Outro with CTA
- Total target runtime with reasoning

**3. First 10 Episode Ideas**
- For each: title, one-sentence description, key talking points
- Strategic ordering (which episodes first and why)
- Pillar episodes vs timely episodes
- 'Gateway' episode for new listeners

**4. Guest Strategy (if interview format)**
- Ideal guest profile criteria
- Where to find guests
- Outreach email template (cold and warm)
- Pre-interview preparation checklist
- Interview question framework (not a rigid list - a framework)
- Post-interview follow-up

**5. Production Workflow**
- Equipment recommendations by budget tier
- Recording setup (software, settings, backup)
- Editing workflow (what to cut, what to keep)
- Show notes template
- Publishing checklist

**6. Launch Strategy**
- Pre-launch runway (4-6 weeks plan)
- Launch day tactics (release 3-5 episodes at once)
- Rating and review generation strategy
- Launch promotion across channels

**7. Growth & Monetization**
- Week-over-week growth tactics
- Cross-promotion and podcast swaps
- Social media content from episodes (clips, quotes, audiograms)
- Newsletter integration
- Monetization timeline and options (sponsors, premium content, services)
- Listener engagement and community building

Be specific with tools, timelines, and tactics. No vague advice.

Pro Tips for Video & Multimedia

  • Specify your platform (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels) since optimal format, length, and style differ dramatically.
  • Include your current audience size and engagement rate so the AI tailors growth strategy to your stage.
  • Ask for specific hooks and opening lines - the first 3 seconds determine whether viewers watch or scroll past.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You are launching a podcast and need everything from concept to launch strategy to growth tactics.
  • You have long-form content (blogs, podcasts, videos) and want to repurpose it into 15+ pieces for other platforms.
  • You need to create video content consistently but struggle with ideas and scripting efficiency.

Expected Results

  • Content calendars with 30 days of specific ideas, hooks, captions, and posting schedules.
  • Launch strategies with pre-launch timelines, day-one tactics, and growth playbooks.
  • Platform-optimized content with format specifications, hashtag strategies, and engagement tactics.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Modify the production notes based on your actual equipment and editing capabilities.
  • Add your monetization model (ads, sponsorships, products, courses) so CTAs align with your revenue strategy.
  • Replace the niche and topic with your actual content focus and the specific subject of this video.
