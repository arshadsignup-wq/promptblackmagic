Online Course Creation Blueprint

Define your expertise and get a complete course creation plan - curriculum design, filming guide, platform selection, and launch strategy.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into any LLM. Use the output to create and sell an online course from idea to launch.

The Prompt

You are an online course creator who has launched 20+ courses generating $5M+ in total revenue, with completion rates 3x the industry average.

Course topic: [WHAT YOU'LL TEACH]
Target student: [WHO WILL TAKE THIS AND THEIR STARTING LEVEL]
Course outcome: [WHAT STUDENTS WILL BE ABLE TO DO AFTER COMPLETING]
Format: [VIDEO/TEXT/MIXED/COHORT-BASED/SELF-PACED]
Platform: [TEACHABLE/THINKIFIC/KAJABI/UDEMY/SKILLSHARE/CUSTOM]

Create a complete course creation blueprint:

**1. Course Design**
- Learning objectives (3-5 specific outcomes, measurable)
- Prerequisite knowledge (what students need to know before starting)
- Course outline:
  - Module structure (5-8 modules, each with clear theme)
  - Lessons per module (3-6 lessons, each 5-15 minutes)
  - Progressive difficulty (each module builds on previous)
  - Milestone projects (application after every 2 modules)

**2. Curriculum Detail (per module)**
- Module title and learning objective
- Lesson breakdown:
  - Lesson title
  - Teaching method (lecture, demo, exercise, case study)
  - Key concepts covered
  - Student activity or exercise
  - Assessment (quiz, project, peer review)
- Resources and downloads (worksheets, templates, checklists)
- Module project (hands-on application)

**3. Content Production**
- Equipment setup for course filming (camera, mic, lighting, teleprompter)
- Filming environment (clean background, good acoustics, consistent)
- Screen recording setup (for demonstrations)
- Slide design template (less text, more visuals)
- Lesson structure: hook, teach, demonstrate, practice, summarize
- Batch filming strategy (film one module per session)
- Editing standards (cuts, graphics, captions)

**4. Student Engagement Design**
- Welcome email and orientation
- Weekly engagement prompts
- Community setup (Circle, Discord, Facebook Group, Slack)
- Office hours or live Q&A schedule
- Peer learning activities
- Progress tracking and celebrations
- Certificate of completion design

**5. Platform and Tech Setup**
- Platform selection criteria and comparison
- Course page design (sales page and course player)
- Drip schedule (all at once vs weekly release)
- Payment processing (one-time, payment plan, subscription)
- Access control and enrollment management
- Mobile experience verification

**6. Pricing Strategy**
- Price research (competitive analysis)
- Value-based pricing methodology
- Tier options (basic, premium, VIP)
- Payment plans (2-pay, 3-pay, monthly)
- Early bird and launch pricing
- Lifetime access vs time-limited access decision

**7. Launch Strategy**
- Pre-launch (4-6 weeks before): email list building, free content, waitlist
- Launch week: live events, limited-time bonuses, social proof
- Post-launch: evergreen funnel setup, ongoing enrollment
- Beta launch option (first cohort at reduced price for testimonials)
- Webinar or challenge launch model

**8. Post-Launch Optimization**
- Student feedback collection
- Completion rate analysis (where students drop off)
- Content updates and improvements
- Upsell and cross-sell opportunities
- Affiliate program setup
- Student success stories for marketing

Design a course people finish, not just buy. Completion rate is the real metric of quality.

Pro Tips for Video & Multimedia

  • Include your equipment setup (phone vs camera, mic type, lighting) so production recommendations match your capabilities.
  • Specify your platform (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels) since optimal format, length, and style differ dramatically.
  • Include your current audience size and engagement rate so the AI tailors growth strategy to your stage.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You are starting a YouTube channel and need professional scripts that keep viewers watching until the end.
  • You want to grow on TikTok or Instagram Reels but do not know what content formats work best for your niche.
  • You are launching a podcast and need everything from concept to launch strategy to growth tactics.

Expected Results

  • Complete video scripts with timestamps, B-roll directions, on-screen text cues, and retention hooks.
  • Content calendars with 30 days of specific ideas, hooks, captions, and posting schedules.
  • Launch strategies with pre-launch timelines, day-one tactics, and growth playbooks.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Adjust the video length and format to match your platform requirements and audience preferences.
  • Include your current subscriber or follower count so growth tactics match your stage.
  • Modify the production notes based on your actual equipment and editing capabilities.
