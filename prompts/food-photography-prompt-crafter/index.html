Food Photography Prompt Crafter

Describe any dish or food product and get mouthwatering AI image prompts with professional styling, lighting, and plating directions.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into your preferred LLM. Replace [DISH] with your food item. Use the generated prompts in any AI image tool.

The Prompt

You are a professional food photographer and stylist who has shot for Bon Appetit, Food & Wine, and major restaurant brands.

Dish/Food: [DISH OR FOOD PRODUCT]
Brand vibe: [RUSTIC/MODERN/LUXURY/HOMESTYLE/HEALTH-FOCUSED]
Use case: [MENU/INSTAGRAM/PACKAGING/COOKBOOK/ADVERTISEMENT]

Create 5 professional food photography prompts:

1. **Overhead Flat Lay** - Top-down shot with complementary ingredients scattered artfully, linen textures, natural light from window
2. **Hero Angle (45-degree)** - Main dish front-center, shallow depth of field, steam/movement captured
3. **Close-Up Texture Shot** - Macro detail showing texture, moisture, freshness cues
4. **Lifestyle Scene** - Hands reaching, mid-bite moment, table setting with drinks and sides
5. **Dark & Moody** - Dark wood surface, dramatic side lighting, chiaroscuro style

For each prompt include:
- Exact prop styling (utensils, napkins, garnishes, surfaces)
- Lighting setup (natural vs artificial, direction, diffusion)
- Color palette that makes the food pop
- Composition technique (leading lines, negative space, rule of odds)
- Camera settings (macro lens, aperture, white balance)
- 10 descriptive keywords for AI generation

Make each prompt a single paragraph ready for Midjourney. Include tips on what to avoid (common AI food photography mistakes).

Pro Tips for Image Generation

  • Reference specific art styles, lighting setups, and color palettes rather than vague descriptors like "beautiful" or "professional" for more consistent results.
  • Specify the aspect ratio and intended use (Instagram post, website hero, print) so the composition works for your platform.
  • Describe the mood with 5-8 specific adjectives rather than generic terms - "warm golden hour glow with soft bokeh" beats "nice lighting" every time.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You are exploring different visual styles for a rebrand and want to rapidly test multiple creative directions.
  • You need product photography for your e-commerce store but cannot afford a professional photographer or studio rental.
  • You are building a social media presence and need consistent, on-brand visual content across multiple platforms.

Expected Results

  • Platform-specific aspect ratios and composition guidelines for each intended use case.
  • Color palette recommendations with hex codes that complement the subject and intended brand aesthetic.
  • Detailed, copy-paste ready prompts for AI image generators with camera settings, lighting specs, and style keywords.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Swap the style preference for your brand aesthetic - minimal, luxury, playful, bold, or organic.
  • Change the platform specification to match where the image will be used (Instagram square, website banner, print ad).
  • Adjust the lighting and mood descriptors to match your brand photography guidelines.
