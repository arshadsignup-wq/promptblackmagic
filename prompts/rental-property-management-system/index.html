Rental Property Management System

Get a complete property management playbook with tenant screening criteria, lease templates, maintenance workflows, and rent collection systems.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into any LLM with your property details. Use the output to systematize your rental management.

The Prompt

You are a property management expert who manages 500+ units with a 98% occupancy rate and minimal legal issues.

Number of units: [HOW MANY RENTALS YOU MANAGE]
Property types: [SINGLE FAMILY/MULTI-FAMILY/CONDO]
Location: [CITY/STATE]
Self-managed or PM company: [WHICH]
Biggest challenge: [WHAT'S CAUSING YOU PAIN]

Build a complete property management system:

**1. Tenant Screening Process**
- Application requirements checklist
- Screening criteria (income ratios, credit score thresholds, rental history)
- Red flags to watch for
- Fair Housing compliance checklist
- Rejection letter template (legally safe)
- Approval and next steps communication

**2. Lease Agreement Essentials**
- Key clauses every lease must have
- Pet policy structure
- Late payment policy and fee schedule
- Maintenance responsibility matrix (landlord vs tenant)
- Lease renewal process and timeline
- Move-in/move-out inspection checklist

**3. Rent Collection System**
- Payment methods to offer
- Due dates and grace period policy
- Late fee escalation schedule
- Partial payment policy
- Non-payment action timeline (notice to pay, eviction filing)
- Communication templates for each stage

**4. Maintenance Management**
- Emergency vs non-emergency classification
- Response time standards
- Vendor selection and management
- Preventive maintenance calendar (monthly, quarterly, annual)
- Tenant maintenance request workflow
- Cost tracking system

**5. Financial Tracking**
- Monthly income/expense tracking template
- Reserve fund guidelines (per unit)
- Tax deduction checklist for rental properties
- Annual budget template
- Rent increase analysis framework

**6. Tenant Communication Templates**
- Welcome packet contents
- Rent increase notice
- Lease violation notice
- Maintenance scheduling messages
- Lease renewal offer
- Move-out instructions and deposit return

Make everything legally aware (note where state-specific laws apply).

Pro Tips for Real Estate

  • Include comparable property data when available so the AI can provide market-aware recommendations.
  • Request jurisdiction-specific legal considerations since real estate laws vary dramatically by state and municipality.
  • Include the property type, price range, and target buyer profile so the AI tailors its output to the right market segment.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You are hosting an open house and want a complete playbook from marketing to follow-up to maximize leads.
  • You manage rental properties and need systems for tenant screening, maintenance, and rent collection.
  • You are analyzing whether to buy or rent in your market and need an objective financial comparison.

Expected Results

  • Legal-aware documentation templates with jurisdiction-specific compliance reminders.
  • MLS-ready listing descriptions with emotional hooks, feature highlights, and SEO keywords.
  • Complete financial analysis with cash flow projections, return metrics, and stress-test scenarios.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Modify the legal and compliance references to match your state or municipality regulations.
  • Add your brokerage branding and contact details for client-facing marketing materials.
  • Replace the property details with your actual address, specs, features, and recent upgrades.
