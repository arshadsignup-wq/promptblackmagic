Real Estate Photography Enhancement Prompt

Describe a property and get AI prompts for enhancing real estate photos - virtual staging, sky replacement, twilight conversion, and seasonal changes.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Use with DALL-E 3 inpainting or Midjourney image blending. Upload existing property photos as reference.

The Prompt

You are a real estate photo editor who processes 500+ listing photos per week for top-producing agents and luxury brokerages.

Property type: [SINGLE FAMILY/CONDO/TOWNHOUSE/LUXURY ESTATE/COMMERCIAL]
Current photo issues: [EMPTY ROOMS/BAD WEATHER/DATED DECOR/POOR LIGHTING/CLUTTERED]
Listing price range: [STARTER/MID-RANGE/LUXURY/ULTRA-LUXURY]
Target buyer demographic: [FIRST-TIME BUYERS/FAMILIES/DOWNSIZERS/INVESTORS/LUXURY BUYERS]

Generate enhancement prompt sets:

**1. Virtual Staging Prompts (4 rooms)**
- Living room: furniture style matching price point and buyer demo
- Primary bedroom: bed size, nightstands, lighting, soft textiles
- Kitchen: countertop styling (fruit bowl, cookbook, flowers)
- Dining room: table setting, chairs, centerpiece
- For each: specify furniture style (modern, transitional, farmhouse), color palette, and realistic shadow placement

**2. Sky Replacement Prompts**
- Overcast to dramatic sunset
- Gray sky to clear blue with wispy clouds
- Daytime to twilight (blue hour) conversion
- Include proper lighting adjustment on building to match new sky

**3. Twilight Conversion**
- Transform daytime exterior to dusk/twilight
- Interior lights glowing warmly through windows
- Landscape lighting on pathways and gardens
- Deep blue sky with warm horizon gradient
- Stars or early evening moon optional

**4. Seasonal Adjustment**
- Winter to spring/summer (green lawn, blooming plants)
- Remove snow, add lush landscaping
- Adjust lighting warmth and shadow angles
- Pool area from empty to inviting

**5. Declutter and Modernize**
- Remove personal items, excess furniture
- Update dated fixtures (describe replacements)
- Fresh paint color suggestions
- Modern lighting fixture swaps

**6. Aerial / Drone Enhancement**
- Property boundary highlighting
- Neighborhood context improvement
- Remove construction or eyesores from surroundings
- Add property markers and lot lines

For each prompt: ensure photorealism is the top priority. Include instructions for matching existing lighting direction, color temperature, perspective, and shadow angles. Buyers must not be able to tell the image was enhanced.

Pro Tips for Image Generation

  • Include negative prompts to exclude unwanted elements like blurry backgrounds, extra fingers, or distorted text in your generated images.
  • Reference specific art styles, lighting setups, and color palettes rather than vague descriptors like "beautiful" or "professional" for more consistent results.
  • Specify the aspect ratio and intended use (Instagram post, website hero, print) so the composition works for your platform.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You need to visualize a concept, mockup, or prototype before investing in professional production.
  • You are exploring different visual styles for a rebrand and want to rapidly test multiple creative directions.
  • You need product photography for your e-commerce store but cannot afford a professional photographer or studio rental.

Expected Results

  • Complete negative prompt suggestions to avoid common AI image artifacts and unwanted elements.
  • Platform-specific aspect ratios and composition guidelines for each intended use case.
  • Color palette recommendations with hex codes that complement the subject and intended brand aesthetic.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Replace the product description with your actual product name, dimensions, materials, and key visual features.
  • Swap the style preference for your brand aesthetic - minimal, luxury, playful, bold, or organic.
  • Change the platform specification to match where the image will be used (Instagram square, website banner, print ad).
