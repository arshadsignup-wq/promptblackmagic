Competitive Battle Card

Name your product and a key competitor. Get a one-page battle card with feature comparison, win/lose scenarios, landmine questions, and objection scripts.

How to Use

Fill in what you know. Share with your sales team. Update monthly as competitors change their offerings.

The Prompt

Create a competitive battle card comparing my product to a key competitor.

My product: [YOUR PRODUCT NAME]
- Key features: [LIST 3-5]
- Pricing model: [e.g., per-user monthly subscription, $X/user/month]
- Target customer: [IDEAL CUSTOMER PROFILE]
- Key differentiators: [WHAT MAKES YOU UNIQUE]

Competitor: [COMPETITOR NAME]
- Their key features (what I know): [LIST WHAT YOU KNOW]
- Their pricing (what I know): [LIST WHAT YOU KNOW]
- Their strengths (from customer feedback): [LIST WHAT YOU HAVE HEARD]

Generate:

1. Quick comparison table: Feature-by-feature comparison with brief notes

2. Where we win: 4-5 specific scenarios where our solution is clearly stronger, with talking points a rep can use in a live conversation

3. Where they win: 2-3 areas where the competitor has an advantage, plus a "neutralizing statement" for each that acknowledges it while redirecting to our strengths

4. Landmine questions: 5 questions our reps can ask the prospect that subtly expose the competitor's weaknesses without trash-talking

5. Objection responses for each of these:
   - "[Competitor] is cheaper"
   - "[Competitor] has feature X that you do not"
   - "We have been using [Competitor] for years and it works fine"
   - "[Competitor]'s support is better"
   Provide word-for-word responses for each.

6. Customer proof points: 3 types of case studies or proof points to create to strengthen our position

Format: One page, scannable, designed for a rep to review in 5 minutes before a competitive call.
