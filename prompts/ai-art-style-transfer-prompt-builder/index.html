Image Generation

AI Art Style Transfer Prompt Builder

Describe any subject and choose an art style to get detailed prompts that recreate famous artistic styles in AI image generators.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into any LLM. Pick your subject and style. Use the output in Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, or DALL-E.

The Prompt

You are an art historian and AI prompt engineer who understands the technical characteristics of every major art movement and style.

Subject: [WHAT YOU WANT TO DEPICT]
Preferred style: [CHOOSE OR SAY 'GIVE ME OPTIONS']

Create 10 AI image prompts, each in a different iconic art style:

1. **Impressionist** (Monet-inspired) - soft brushstrokes, light play, pastel palette
2. **Pop Art** (Warhol-inspired) - bold colors, halftone dots, graphic contrast
3. **Art Nouveau** - flowing organic lines, floral motifs, decorative borders
4. **Cyberpunk/Neon** - neon lights, rain-slicked streets, futuristic dystopia
5. **Japanese Ukiyo-e** - woodblock print style, flat color, wave patterns
6. **Surrealism** (Dali-inspired) - dreamlike, melting forms, impossible physics
7. **Minimalist/Bauhaus** - geometric shapes, primary colors, clean lines
8. **Dark Fantasy** - dramatic lighting, gothic architecture, rich textures
9. **Watercolor Illustration** - soft washes, visible paper texture, gentle bleeds
10. **Pixel Art/Retro Gaming** - 8-bit or 16-bit style, limited palette, nostalgic

For each prompt include:
- Art movement context (what defines this style technically)
- 10-15 specific style keywords for AI generators
- Color palette (5 hex codes per style)
- Composition approach specific to that art movement
- Recommended AI model and settings
- Negative prompts to avoid common AI artifacts in that style

Pro Tips for Image Generation

  • Include negative prompts to exclude unwanted elements like blurry backgrounds, extra fingers, or distorted text in your generated images.
  • Reference specific art styles, lighting setups, and color palettes rather than vague descriptors like "beautiful" or "professional" for more consistent results.
  • Specify the aspect ratio and intended use (Instagram post, website hero, print) so the composition works for your platform.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You are designing marketing materials and need eye-catching visuals that match a specific brand aesthetic.
  • You need to visualize a concept, mockup, or prototype before investing in professional production.
  • You are exploring different visual styles for a rebrand and want to rapidly test multiple creative directions.

Expected Results

  • Complete negative prompt suggestions to avoid common AI image artifacts and unwanted elements.
  • Platform-specific aspect ratios and composition guidelines for each intended use case.
  • Color palette recommendations with hex codes that complement the subject and intended brand aesthetic.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Modify the camera and lens specs to match the look you want - wider for environmental, tighter for product detail.
  • Replace the product description with your actual product name, dimensions, materials, and key visual features.
  • Swap the style preference for your brand aesthetic - minimal, luxury, playful, bold, or organic.
