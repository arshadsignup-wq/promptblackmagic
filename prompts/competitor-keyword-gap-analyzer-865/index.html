Competitor Keyword Gap Analyzer

Identify keyword opportunities your competitors rank for that you do not, and build a content plan to close those gaps.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into any LLM. List your competitors and current keyword focus. Use the output to prioritize content that captures untapped search traffic.

The Prompt

You are an SEO strategist specializing in competitive keyword analysis who has helped 200+ websites identify and capture thousands of untapped keyword opportunities from competitors.

[YOUR WEBSITE]: Your domain
[COMPETITOR 1]: Competitor domain
[COMPETITOR 2]: Competitor domain
[COMPETITOR 3]: Competitor domain (optional)
[YOUR CORE TOPICS]: Main topics you create content about
[CURRENT MONTHLY TRAFFIC]: Approximate organic traffic
[CONTENT CAPACITY]: How many articles/pages you can publish per month

Conduct a comprehensive keyword gap analysis:

**1. Competitor Content Mapping**
For each competitor:
- Estimated organic traffic and keyword count
- Top-performing pages and their target keywords
- Content types they publish (blog, tools, resources, guides)
- Content depth and quality assessment
- Publishing frequency and cadence

**2. Keyword Gap Identification**
- Keywords all competitors rank for that you do not
- Keywords with high volume and low difficulty you are missing
- Long-tail keyword clusters competitors cover
- Question-based keywords in your niche
- Informational vs. commercial intent gaps

**3. Opportunity Scoring**
For each gap category:
- Search volume potential
- Keyword difficulty assessment
- Business relevance and conversion potential
- Content effort required
- Competitive advantage assessment
- Priority score (1-10)

**4. Content Plan**
- Top 20 keyword gaps to target first (ranked by priority)
- Content type recommendation for each (blog post, guide, tool, landing page)
- Target word count and content depth
- Internal linking opportunities from existing content
- Content cluster strategy for topic authority

**5. Quick Win Opportunities**
- Existing pages to optimize for gap keywords
- Featured snippet opportunities
- People Also Ask targets
- Image and video SEO gaps

**6. Execution Roadmap**
- Month 1-3 content calendar
- Content brief template for gap-filling content
- Measurement plan and expected timeline for results
- Ongoing competitive monitoring recommendations

Pro Tips for Content & SEO

  • Always include your target keyword and search intent (informational, commercial, transactional) when prompting for SEO content.
  • Ask the AI to structure content with proper H2/H3 headings that include semantic variations of your target keyword.
  • Specify the content format you need - listicle, how-to guide, comparison, or pillar page - as each has different structural requirements.

When to Use This Prompt

  • Your existing content is losing rankings and you need to refresh it with updated information and better structure.
  • You are building a content cluster around a pillar topic and need supporting articles that strengthen topical authority.
  • Your site has thin pages that Google is ignoring, and you need to expand them with valuable, search-optimized content.

Expected Results

  • Meta titles and descriptions that balance keyword targeting with click-through rate optimization.
  • Content briefs with target word count, competitor analysis, and recommended subtopics to cover.
  • Content refresh recommendations that identify outdated sections and suggest updated information.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Include URLs of your existing related content so the AI can suggest natural internal linking opportunities.
  • Modify the content format (listicle, how-to, comparison) based on what SERP analysis shows Google prefers.
  • Swap the audience description with your actual reader persona including expertise level and search intent.
