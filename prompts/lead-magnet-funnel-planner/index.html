Lead Magnet Funnel Planner

A strategic planner for selecting and positioning the right lead magnet, designing its landing page, and mapping the nurturing email sequence.

By Arshad Hossain

How to Use

Copy & paste the prompt below into your preferred LLM. Unless a specific AI model is mentioned, you can use whichever you prefer.

The Prompt

You are an expert lead generation strategist. I’m trying to build a lead funnel for my [service-based business], and I’d like to offer a compelling lead magnet. Please advise on the best format for the magnet, how to position it on my landing page, and which email drip sequence would nurture new leads most effectively. Make sure to ask me questions about my target audience, core offer, and the kind of content I can provide.

Why "Lead Magnet Funnel Planner" Works

What separates "Lead Magnet Funnel Planner" from an off-the-cuff AI question is precision. It applies role assignment and output formatting and audience specification, which gives the model enough direction to produce buyer-psychology-informed messaging that handles objections and moves deals forward. Your output will be buyer-psychology-informed messaging that handles objections and moves deals forward - the difference between useful AI assistance and a response you immediately delete.

Pro Tips for Using "Lead Magnet Funnel Planner"

These sales tips will help you get stronger results when using "Lead Magnet Funnel Planner" and similar prompts in this category.

  • Always include your prospect's industry and pain points in the prompt - generic sales messages get ignored, personalized ones get meetings.
  • Specify whether you need cold outreach, follow-up, or closing copy - each stage requires fundamentally different messaging.
  • Include your product pricing tier so the AI can calibrate value propositions appropriate to the deal size.

When to Use "Lead Magnet Funnel Planner"

"Lead Magnet Funnel Planner" is particularly useful in these situations. If any of these scenarios sound familiar, this prompt will save you significant time.

  • You are cold-emailing a list of 200 prospects and need personalized outreach templates that get responses, not spam reports.
  • You are preparing a proposal for an enterprise client and need a persuasive executive summary that leads with ROI.
  • You are onboarding a new sales rep and need a playbook that covers discovery calls, demos, and closing techniques.

What You Will Get from "Lead Magnet Funnel Planner"

When you use "Lead Magnet Funnel Planner" with ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini, here is what to expect in the AI output.

  • Discovery call question frameworks that uncover budget, authority, need, and timeline efficiently.
  • Follow-up sequences with varied touchpoints across email, phone, and social for multi-channel outreach.
  • Objection-handling scripts that reframe concerns into buying reasons using specific value evidence.

How to Customize "Lead Magnet Funnel Planner"

Adapt "Lead Magnet Funnel Planner" to your specific situation by modifying these key areas. The more context you add, the better the results.

  • Adjust the deal size references to match your pricing tier so the AI calibrates urgency and formality.
  • Modify the follow-up timeline to match your actual sales cycle length: one week for SMB, months for enterprise.
  • Replace the prospect industry placeholder with your actual target vertical for relevant pain point messaging.
