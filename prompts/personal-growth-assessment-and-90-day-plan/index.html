Personal Growth Assessment and 90-Day Plan

Evaluate your current life areas and get a structured 90-day personal development plan with specific goals, habits, and accountability systems.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into any LLM. Complete the self-assessment honestly, then follow the generated plan.

The Prompt

You are a personal development coach who has helped 1,000+ professionals achieve breakthrough growth by combining evidence-based psychology with practical systems.

Focus area: [CAREER/RELATIONSHIPS/HEALTH/FINANCES/MINDSET/ALL]
Current life satisfaction (1-10): [RATING]
Biggest obstacle: [WHAT'S HOLDING YOU BACK]
Available time for growth: [HOURS PER WEEK]
Learning style: [READING/LISTENING/DOING/VISUAL/SOCIAL]

Create a comprehensive personal development plan:

**1. Life Audit (Wheel of Life Assessment)**
Rate yourself (1-10) and identify the gap:
- Career and professional growth
- Financial health
- Physical health and energy
- Mental health and emotional wellbeing
- Relationships and social life
- Family
- Fun, recreation, and hobbies
- Personal growth and learning
- Contribution and giving back
- Physical environment (home, workspace)

**2. 90-Day Goal Setting**
Using the SMART+WHY framework:
- Goal 1 (highest priority area): specific, measurable, with deadline
  - Why this matters deeply to you
  - What life looks like when achieved
  - Obstacles to anticipate
  - Resources and support needed
- Goal 2 (secondary area): same format
- Goal 3 (maintenance area): same format

**3. Daily System Design**
- Morning routine (optimized for energy and focus)
- Non-negotiable daily habits (3-5 keystone habits)
- Evening review and planning ritual
- Time blocking template for growth activities
- Energy management (when to do what type of work)

**4. Weekly Rhythm**
- Weekly review questions (what worked, what didn't, what's next)
- Weekly planning session structure
- Social and relationship commitments
- Physical activity schedule
- Learning and skill development blocks
- Rest and recovery time (non-negotiable)

**5. Skill Development Plan**
- Skills to develop (aligned with goals)
- Learning resources (books, courses, mentors, communities)
- Practice schedule and milestones
- Application opportunities (how to use new skills immediately)
- Progress measurement

**6. Accountability System**
- Self-accountability tools (habit tracker, journal prompts)
- External accountability (partner, coach, community)
- Weekly check-in questions
- Monthly milestone review
- How to get back on track when you slip

**7. Mindset Work**
- Limiting beliefs to address (identify and reframe)
- Affirmation practice (specific, evidence-based)
- Gratitude practice design
- Journaling prompts for self-awareness
- Meditation or mindfulness approach

**8. Environment Design**
- Physical environment changes to support goals
- Digital environment optimization (apps, screen time, information diet)
- Social environment audit (who lifts you up, who drains you)
- Trigger and habit loop design
- Temptation reduction strategies

Be direct and practical. This plan should feel like a personal operating system, not a motivational poster.

Pro Tips for Personal Development

  • Include what you have already tried so the AI avoids recommending approaches that did not work for you.
  • Request accountability structures and failure recovery protocols - plans without follow-through systems rarely stick.
  • Specify your preferred tools (Notion, paper, apps) so the AI designs systems that fit your actual workflow.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You are considering a career change and need a structured plan for transitioning without burning bridges.
  • You are experiencing burnout and need practical recovery strategies that go beyond generic self-care advice.
  • You want to build better habits but previous attempts have failed, and you need a system that sticks.

Expected Results

  • Self-assessment frameworks that identify root causes rather than surface-level symptoms.
  • Accountability structures with check-in schedules, progress metrics, and failure recovery protocols.
  • Structured daily, weekly, and quarterly systems with specific templates, questions, and tracking methods.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Include your preferred tools and methods so the system fits your existing workflow.
  • Modify the accountability structures based on whether you have a partner, coach, or are self-directed.
  • Add your past experiences with similar systems, noting what worked and what did not.
