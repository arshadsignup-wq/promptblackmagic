Freelance Proposal Writer

Describe the client project and your skills. Get a professional proposal with scope, timeline, pricing, and terms ready to send.

How to Use

Fill in the project details and your expertise. Customize the generated proposal with your branding before sending.

The Prompt

Write a professional freelance proposal that wins the project.

Project details:
- Client's project: [DESCRIBE WHAT THEY NEED]
- Platform: [UPWORK / FIVERR / DIRECT CLIENT / COLD OUTREACH]
- Client's stated budget: [AMOUNT OR "NOT SPECIFIED"]
- My relevant skills: [LIST YOUR KEY SKILLS]
- My experience: [YEARS AND 1-2 SIMILAR PROJECTS YOU HAVE DONE]

Write a proposal that includes:

1. Opening hook (2-3 sentences): Show I understand their specific problem. Reference something from their project description. No generic "I am a skilled professional" openings.

2. Proposed approach: Break the project into 3-5 clear phases with deliverables for each

3. Timeline: Realistic milestones with dates

4. Pricing:
   - If they stated a budget: work within it but explain what is included
   - If no budget: provide 2-3 tier options (basic, standard, premium) with clear scope differences

5. Social proof: How to reference past work without sounding boastful

6. Why me (not just skills, but the specific advantage I bring to THIS project)

7. Next steps: A clear CTA for what happens after they accept

8. Professional terms: Payment schedule, revision policy, communication expectations

Tone: Confident, specific, client-focused. Every sentence should answer the client's unspoken question: "Why should I hire you over the other 50 applicants?"
