Legal Research Summary Generator

Structure legal research on any topic with case law analysis, statutory interpretation, and practical implications organized for quick reference.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste this prompt with your legal research question into any LLM. Always verify all case citations and statutory references independently. AI can hallucinate legal citations.

The Prompt

You are a legal research assistant helping organize and analyze legal materials.

Research question: [SPECIFIC LEGAL QUESTION OR ISSUE]
Jurisdiction: [FEDERAL / STATE - SPECIFY / INTERNATIONAL]
Area of law: [CONTRACT / TORT / EMPLOYMENT / IP / CRIMINAL / REGULATORY / CORPORATE]
Purpose: [CLIENT MEMO / COURT FILING / POLICY ANALYSIS / EDUCATIONAL]

Structure a legal research summary:

1. **Issue statement:** Frame the legal question precisely using IRAC format (Issue, Rule, Application, Conclusion)
2. **Applicable statutes and regulations:**
   - Primary statutes with section numbers
   - Relevant regulations and administrative guidance
   - Recent amendments or proposed changes
3. **Case law analysis:**
   - Leading cases establishing the current rule
   - Recent cases showing how courts apply the rule
   - Circuit splits or conflicting authority (if any)
   - For each case: citation, key holding, and relevance to the research question
4. **Practical implications:**
   - How this law applies to the specific situation
   - Risk factors and potential liability exposure
   - Recommended course of action
5. **Open questions:** Areas of legal uncertainty or evolving law

CRITICAL DISCLAIMER: AI-generated legal research may contain hallucinated citations and inaccurate statements of law. Every citation, statute number, and legal conclusion must be independently verified using official legal databases (Westlaw, LexisNexis, court websites). This is a research starting point, not legal advice.

Pro Tips for Legal

  • Always include a disclaimer that AI-generated legal content is not legal advice and must be reviewed by a licensed attorney.
  • Specify the jurisdiction (federal, state, country) since laws vary dramatically and generic legal information can be misleading.
  • Ask the AI to flag any citations or case references for independent verification - AI models can hallucinate legal citations.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You are a law student briefing cases for class and need consistent, well-structured case briefs with holdings and reasoning.
  • Your client has a dispute and you need to draft a demand letter with proper legal structure and professional tone.
  • You are setting up a new law practice and need client intake questionnaires tailored to your practice area.

Expected Results

  • Contract review analyses with risk ratings, missing clause identification, and suggested negotiation language.
  • Case briefs with procedural history, holdings, reasoning, and significance analysis in consistent format.
  • Demand letter drafts with proper legal structure, factual recitation, and professional tone.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Modify the citation format to match your court or firm requirements (Bluebook, ALWD, local rules).
  • Add your firm branding and contact information placeholders for client-facing documents.
  • Replace the jurisdiction placeholder with your specific state, federal circuit, or country for jurisdiction-accurate analysis.
