SWOT Analysis Generator

Describe your business or product and get a detailed SWOT analysis with actionable strategies for each quadrant.

How to Use

Copy the prompt and describe your business, product, or project.

The Prompt

Create a detailed SWOT analysis for:

Business/Product: [NAME]
Industry: [INDUSTRY]
Current stage: [STARTUP / GROWING / ESTABLISHED]
Main competitors: [LIST 2-3]

For each quadrant, provide:

Strengths (5 items):
- What you do well, with evidence
- Strategy: How to leverage each strength

Weaknesses (5 items):
- Honest assessment of gaps
- Strategy: How to address or mitigate each

Opportunities (5 items):
- External trends you can capitalize on
- Strategy: Specific action to seize each opportunity

Threats (5 items):
- External risks to watch
- Strategy: How to defend against each threat

End with: Top 3 strategic priorities based on this analysis.
