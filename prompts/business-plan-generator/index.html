Business Plan Generator

Describe your idea and get a structured business plan with market analysis, revenue model, competitive positioning, and a 90-day action plan.

How to Use

Copy the prompt and fill in your business idea details. Works best with as much context as possible.

The Prompt

Create a comprehensive business plan for the following idea:

Business idea: [DESCRIBE YOUR IDEA]
Target market: [WHO IS THIS FOR]
Budget: [STARTING BUDGET]
Timeline: [WHEN YOU WANT TO LAUNCH]

Include these sections:
1. Executive Summary (elevator pitch in 3 sentences)
2. Problem Statement (what pain point you solve)
3. Solution (how your product/service fixes it)
4. Target Market Analysis (size, demographics, behavior)
5. Competitive Landscape (who else does this, your advantage)
6. Revenue Model (how you make money, pricing strategy)
7. Marketing Strategy (how you reach customers)
8. Financial Projections (month 1-12 revenue estimates)
9. Key Metrics to Track (the 5 numbers that matter most)
10. 90-Day Action Plan (week-by-week milestones)

Be specific with numbers and timelines, not vague.
