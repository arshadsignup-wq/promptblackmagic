YouTube Shorts Script Generator

Write scripts for YouTube Shorts that hook viewers in the first second and drive subscribers in under 60 seconds.

By Arshad Hossain

How to Use

Copy & paste the prompt below into your preferred LLM. Unless a specific AI model is mentioned, you can use whichever you prefer.

The Prompt

You are a short-form video strategist. Write YouTube Shorts scripts that get views and subscribers.

Video details:
- Niche: [YOUR CHANNEL NICHE]
- Topic: [SPECIFIC VIDEO TOPIC]
- Style: [TALKING HEAD / VOICEOVER / SCREEN RECORDING / DEMONSTRATION / SKIT]
- Target viewer: [WHO WATCHES YOUR CONTENT?]
- Duration: [15 / 30 / 45 / 60 SECONDS]
- Goal: [VIEWS / SUBSCRIBERS / TRAFFIC TO LONG-FORM / BRAND AWARENESS]

Deliver:
1. **3 Complete Scripts** using different hook styles:
   - Each script includes: Hook (first 1-3 seconds), content (value delivery), CTA (last 3-5 seconds)
   - Word count calibrated to duration (roughly 2 words per second for speaking)
   - Visual/action notes in [brackets] throughout
2. **Hook Options:** 5 opening hooks for this topic:
   - The Question ("Did you know...")
   - The Contradiction ("Everyone thinks X, but actually...")
   - The Promise ("In 30 seconds, you'll know how to...")
   - The Demo ("Watch what happens when...")
   - The Urgency ("Stop scrolling if you...")
3. **Retention Techniques:** Pattern interrupts, visual changes, and pacing notes to keep viewers past the 3-second mark
4. **CTA Scripts:** "Follow for part 2," "Subscribe for more," "Comment your answer" — 5 options
5. **Caption/Title:** 5 options for the video title/caption. Relevant hashtags (3-5)
6. **Thumbnail Text:** If applicable, the overlay text for the first frame
7. **Series Potential:** How to turn this into a recurring format for consistent posting

The first second determines if someone watches. The last second determines if they subscribe.

Why "YouTube Shorts Script Generator" Works

What makes "YouTube Shorts Script Generator" worth using over writing your own prompt is the engineering behind it. The tone calibration and success criteria and depth requirements built into this well-structured prompt took multiple iterations to refine. Expect content formatted for your specific platform that encourages saves, shares, and meaningful engagement. The constraints in this prompt prevent the model from falling back on vague, unhelpful responses.

Pro Tips for Using "YouTube Shorts Script Generator"

These social media tips will help you get stronger results when using "YouTube Shorts Script Generator" and similar prompts in this category.

  • Specify your brand personality (witty, authoritative, empathetic) so every post sounds authentically you, not generically AI.
  • Ask the AI to include relevant hashtag suggestions - the right hashtags can increase reach by 30% or more on platforms like Instagram.
  • Request content calendar batches - asking for 5-7 posts at once creates thematic consistency across your feed.

When to Use "YouTube Shorts Script Generator"

"YouTube Shorts Script Generator" is particularly useful in these situations. If any of these scenarios sound familiar, this prompt will save you significant time.

  • You are planning a product launch campaign and need a coordinated social media rollout across multiple platforms.
  • You want to repurpose a long-form blog post into platform-specific social content without sounding repetitive.
  • You need a week of content across Instagram, LinkedIn, and X but only have an hour to plan and draft everything.

What You Will Get from "YouTube Shorts Script Generator"

When you use "YouTube Shorts Script Generator" with ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini, here is what to expect in the AI output.

  • Brand voice guidelines that maintain consistency across platforms while adapting to each audience.
  • Platform-specific content formatted to each network's character limits, tone, and content style.
  • Content calendar drafts with post themes, captions, and suggested posting times for maximum engagement.

How to Customize "YouTube Shorts Script Generator"

Adapt "YouTube Shorts Script Generator" to your specific situation by modifying these key areas. The more context you add, the better the results.

  • Adjust the hashtag suggestions by replacing generic tags with niche-specific ones relevant to your industry.
  • Include links to your top-performing past posts so the AI can identify patterns in what resonates with your audience.
  • Specify which platform you are posting to since Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, and X each need different formats.
