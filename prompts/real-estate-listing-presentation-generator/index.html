Real Estate Listing Presentation Generator

Input property details and get a complete listing presentation - property description, marketing plan, pricing strategy, and seller communication templates.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into any LLM with the property details. Use the output for your listing appointment and marketing package.

The Prompt

You are a top-producing real estate agent who has listed and sold 200+ homes using data-driven pricing and marketing strategies that consistently sell above asking price.

Property address: [ADDRESS OR NEIGHBORHOOD]
Property type: [SINGLE FAMILY/CONDO/TOWNHOUSE/LUXURY/MULTI-FAMILY]
Bedrooms/bathrooms: [BED/BATH COUNT]
Square footage: [SIZE]
Unique features: [WHAT MAKES THIS PROPERTY SPECIAL]
Seller motivation: [RELOCATING/UPGRADING/DOWNSIZING/DIVORCE/ESTATE/INVESTOR]

Create a complete listing presentation:

**1. Property Description (MLS-Ready)**
- Headline (attention-grabbing, searchable)
- Full description (250-350 words): opening hook, key features, lifestyle benefits, neighborhood highlights, call to action
- Feature highlights (bulleted for quick scanning)
- SEO-optimized for real estate portals
- Avoid: all caps, excessive punctuation, vague terms

**2. Pricing Strategy**
- Comparative Market Analysis (CMA) framework
- Pricing psychology (just below round numbers, price band strategy)
- Days-on-market analysis for the area
- Price reduction schedule (if needed, when and how much)
- Multiple offer strategy if priced to generate competition

**3. Marketing Plan**
- Professional photography shot list (rooms, angles, time of day)
- Virtual tour / video walkthrough script
- Social media campaign (platforms, content calendar, ad budget)
- Email marketing to buyer agent database
- Open house strategy (broker tour, public open, twilight event)
- Print marketing (flyers, postcards, signage)
- Coming soon / pre-marketing strategy
- Syndication channels (Zillow, Realtor.com, local MLS)

**4. Staging Recommendations**
- Room-by-room staging priorities
- Decluttering checklist
- Repair and touch-up priorities (highest ROI improvements)
- Curb appeal improvements
- Staging budget estimate

**5. Showing Strategy**
- Showing instructions for buyers' agents
- Property highlights to call out during showings
- Feedback collection process
- Offer deadline strategy (if applicable)

**6. Seller Communication**
- Weekly update template
- Showing feedback summary format
- Price reduction conversation script
- Offer presentation format
- Negotiation strategy framework
- Closing timeline checklist

**7. Post-Sale**
- Closing checklist for seller
- Moving timeline
- Referral request approach
- Review request template

Write all copy in a tone that sells the lifestyle, not just the property. Include specific language that resonates with your target buyer demographic.

Pro Tips for Real Estate

  • Request jurisdiction-specific legal considerations since real estate laws vary dramatically by state and municipality.
  • Include the property type, price range, and target buyer profile so the AI tailors its output to the right market segment.
  • Specify your local market conditions (buyer/seller market, inventory levels) for more relevant pricing and strategy advice.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You are hosting an open house and want a complete playbook from marketing to follow-up to maximize leads.
  • You manage rental properties and need systems for tenant screening, maintenance, and rent collection.
  • You are analyzing whether to buy or rent in your market and need an objective financial comparison.

Expected Results

  • MLS-ready listing descriptions with emotional hooks, feature highlights, and SEO keywords.
  • Complete financial analysis with cash flow projections, return metrics, and stress-test scenarios.
  • Multi-channel marketing copy (social media, email, print) tailored to the property and buyer profile.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Modify the legal and compliance references to match your state or municipality regulations.
  • Add your brokerage branding and contact details for client-facing marketing materials.
  • Replace the property details with your actual address, specs, features, and recent upgrades.
