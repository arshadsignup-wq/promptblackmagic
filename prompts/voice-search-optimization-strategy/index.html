Voice Search Optimization Strategy

Optimize your content for voice search queries from Alexa, Siri, Google Assistant, and other voice-enabled devices.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Paste into any LLM. Describe your content and audience. Use the strategy to capture the growing voice search audience.

The Prompt

You are a voice search optimization specialist who has helped websites capture featured snippets and position zero results that voice assistants read aloud, increasing organic visibility by 30-50%.

[YOUR WEBSITE]: Domain and niche
[TARGET AUDIENCE]: Demographics and device usage
[CURRENT CONTENT TYPES]: Blog posts, FAQs, product pages, etc.
[TOP KEYWORDS]: Your primary target keywords
[BUSINESS TYPE]: Local / National / E-commerce / SaaS

Create a voice search optimization strategy:

**1. Voice Search Query Analysis**
- How voice queries differ from typed queries
- Question patterns by intent (who, what, where, when, how, why)
- Conversational long-tail keyword identification
- Local voice search patterns ("near me" variations)
- Voice shopping query patterns

**2. Content Optimization for Voice**
- FAQ page optimization (voice-friendly Q&A format)
- Featured snippet targeting techniques
- Conversational writing style guidelines
- Direct answer formatting (position zero optimization)
- Concise answer + detailed follow-up structure

**3. Structured Data for Voice**
- Speakable schema markup implementation
- FAQ schema for voice responses
- HowTo schema for procedural queries
- Local Business schema for "near me" queries
- Product schema for voice commerce

**4. Technical Optimization**
- Page speed requirements for voice results
- HTTPS requirement verification
- Mobile-first optimization checklist
- Domain authority improvement tactics
- Content freshness signals

**5. Content Creation Plan**
- 15 voice-optimized content pieces to create
- Question-based content templates
- Conversational FAQ expansion strategy
- "People Also Ask" content mining
- Voice search content brief template

**6. Measurement and Iteration**
- Tracking voice search visibility
- Featured snippet win rate monitoring
- Voice search traffic estimation
- Testing and iteration process
- Emerging voice search trends to watch

Pro Tips for Content & SEO

  • Request the AI to include internal linking suggestions that connect the content to related pages on your site.
  • Include your target word count and competitor URLs so the AI creates content that can realistically compete in search results.
  • Always include your target keyword and search intent (informational, commercial, transactional) when prompting for SEO content.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You are creating product descriptions for an e-commerce site and need SEO-friendly copy that also converts browsers into buyers.
  • You need meta titles and descriptions for 50 pages and want each one to be unique, keyword-rich, and click-worthy.
  • You are publishing a blog post targeting a competitive keyword and need content that can realistically rank on page one.

Expected Results

  • Internal linking maps that connect new content to existing pages for stronger topical authority.
  • Long-form content structured with SEO-friendly headings, internal links, and semantic keyword variations.
  • Meta titles and descriptions that balance keyword targeting with click-through rate optimization.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Replace the target keyword placeholder with your actual primary and secondary keywords from keyword research.
  • Adjust the content length to match what currently ranks on page one for your target search query.
  • Add your brand voice guidelines so the content sounds like your company rather than generic SEO filler.
