LinkedIn Job Search Strategy Prompt

Build a complete LinkedIn job search strategy including profile optimization, networking scripts, content plan, and application tracking system.

By The Prompt Black Magic Team

How to Use

Fill in your career details and paste into any LLM. Follow the strategy for 30 days for best results.

The Prompt

You are a career strategist and LinkedIn expert. Build me a complete job search strategy.

Target role: [JOB TITLE YOU WANT]
Current role: [YOUR CURRENT POSITION]
Industry: [TARGET INDUSTRY]
Experience level: [YEARS OF EXPERIENCE]
Location preference: [REMOTE / HYBRID / SPECIFIC CITY]
Timeline: [HOW SOON YOU NEED A NEW ROLE]

Create a comprehensive LinkedIn job search strategy:

1. **Profile optimization:**
   - Headline formula (not just your job title)
   - About section draft (first person, keyword-rich, tells your story)
   - Experience section rewrites with quantified achievements
   - Skills to add and endorse
   - Featured section strategy
   - Profile photo and banner guidance

2. **Keyword strategy:** The 15-20 keywords recruiters search for your target role. Where to place each one.

3. **Networking plan:**
   - Connection request template for recruiters
   - Connection request template for hiring managers
   - Follow-up message after connecting (not pitchy)
   - How to engage with target company employees' posts
   - Warm introduction request script

4. **Content strategy (4 weeks):**
   - Week 1-4 content calendar: what to post and when
   - Post templates that demonstrate expertise without being salesy
   - Comment strategy for visibility on industry posts

5. **Application system:**
   - Daily/weekly activity targets
   - Application tracking spreadsheet template
   - Follow-up timeline after applying
   - Thank you and post-interview follow-up templates

6. **30-day action plan:** Day-by-day breakdown of what to do

Make this specific to my target role and industry. Generic advice wastes job search time.

Pro Tips for Resume & Career

  • Request the AI to use strong action verbs at the start of each bullet point - words like "spearheaded," "optimized," and "launched" grab recruiter attention.
  • Include the industry you're targeting so the AI uses relevant jargon and highlights transferable skills that matter in that field.
  • Always include the specific job title and key requirements from the job posting when prompting for resume help - ATS systems scan for exact keyword matches.

When to Use This Prompt

  • You are negotiating a salary offer and need data-backed talking points that justify your target compensation.
  • You are a recent graduate with limited work experience and need to showcase projects, internships, and skills strategically.
  • You want to update your LinkedIn profile so it attracts recruiter searches for specific job titles in your field.

Expected Results

  • Salary negotiation scripts with market data references and value-based justification language.
  • Cover letter drafts that connect your specific experience to the employer's stated needs and culture.
  • LinkedIn profile sections optimized for recruiter search visibility in your target role.

How to Customize This Prompt

  • Include the company name and its stated values so the cover letter references what matters to that employer.
  • Modify the skills section to match the exact requirements listed in the job description you are applying to.
  • Replace the generic job title with the exact title from the job posting to improve ATS keyword matching.
